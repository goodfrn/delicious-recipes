ingredients:
- "6 cups cold water"
- "3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice"
- "6 large ripe pears peeled cored and diced"
- "2/3 cup coconut sugar"
- "2/3 cup packed brown sugar"
- "1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon"
- "1 teaspoon apple pie spice"
- "1/2 teaspoon ground fresh ginger powder"
- "1 box yellow cake mix dry not prepared"
- "1 cup sliced cold ghee"
instructions:
- "Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Generously grease a 9 by 13 inch baking pan with butter or oil spray."
- "In a large bowl, combine cold water with lemon juice; lemon controls enzymatic browning and balances pear sweetness."
- "Immediately drop peeled, cored, diced pears into the lemon water to prevent discoloration and slightly firm their texture."
- "Use a colander to drain pears completely. Excess water dilutes sugars and can cause soggy crust; rinse briefly if needed to remove grit."
- "Return pears to the bowl. Sprinkle coconut sugar and brown sugar evenly. Add cinnamon, apple pie spice, and fresh ground ginger powder."
- "Use a large spoon or spatula to toss gently but thoroughly until every piece is well coated. This ensures spices cling and distribute evenly."
- "Transfer the pear mixture to the prepared baking dish. Spread out evenly; avoid overcrowding in one spot for uniform baking."
- "Sprinkle dry yellow cake mix evenly atop the pears. A light pat or gentle shake helps it settle into mound, but keep layer loose enough to let moisture seep."
- "Distribute cold ghee slices on top, spacing evenly. Cold fat drops slowly melt, creating layered tender crust pockets — different from butter pats."
- "Place the pan in the oven. Bake anywhere from 40 to 50 minutes. Look for deep golden-brown top, slightly puffed cake mix, and bubbling fruit sides."
- "Don’t rely solely on time. Texture is key; center should feel set yet soft beneath crust. Edges may brown faster; ensure even cooking by rotating pan halfway."
- "Remove from oven once desired crust and bubbling achieved. Let cool briefly to thicken juices for easier serving. Serve warm, potentially with cream or ice cream."
