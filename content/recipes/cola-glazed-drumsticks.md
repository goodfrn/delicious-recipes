ingredients:
- "8 chicken drumsticks, skin-on"
- "3 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided"
- "1 medium onion, finely chopped"
- "4 garlic cloves, minced"
- "1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated"
- "2 cans (12 fl oz each) Cola or Pepsi"
- "1⁄4 cup low sodium soy sauce"
- "1 heaping tablespoon dark brown sugar"
- "1 teaspoon ground black pepper"
- "1 teaspoon kosher salt"
instructions:
- "Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a 4 to 5-quart Dutch oven over medium-high. Oil should shimmer but not smoke."
- "Add drumsticks in a single layer. Sear 4 minutes per side until golden brown and skin crisps a bit. Important: don’t crowd the pan or they’ll steam instead of brown."
- "Remove drumsticks, set aside. Wipe pan if too much fat or burnt bits."
- "Add remaining oil. Toss in onions, sauté uncovered stirring frequently, 3 minutes. Watch for translucent edges and sweet onion aroma. Keep medium-high heat to avoid limp mush."
- "Add garlic and ginger. Stir 1 to 2 minutes. Garlic must soften but not burn—yellow flecks signal trouble."
- "Pour in cola, soy sauce, brown sugar, black pepper, and salt. Stir well, let sauce bubble and reduce uncovered for 5 to 6 minutes. Sauce thickens subtly, slightly syrupy but still fluid."
- "Lower heat to medium. Nestle drumsticks back in sauce, skin side up. Simmer uncovered 25 minutes, turning drumsticks halfway through to cook evenly."
- "After 25 minutes, lift drumsticks out with tongs, set on plate. Turn heat higher to medium-high. Boil sauce uncovered 6 to 10 minutes until syrupy and coats spoon thickly. Stir often to avoid scorching—dark sticky bits are flavor boosters but can burn quick."
- "Remove from heat. Spoon thickened glaze generously over drumsticks. Serve immediately with rice or steamed greens."
