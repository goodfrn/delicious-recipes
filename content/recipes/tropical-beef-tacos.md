ingredients:
- "8 small corn or flour tortillas"
- "3 tablespoons avocado oil or grapeseed oil"
- "1 teaspoon ground turmeric"
- "1 teaspoon ground cumin"
- "1.25 pounds skirt steak or flank steak, trimmed of large fat"
- "fine sea salt to taste"
- "freshly cracked black pepper to taste"
- "1 cup mango salsa or pineapple salsa (substitute diced peach if mango unavailable)"
- "1 cup thinly shredded red cabbage"
- "1 head Bibb lettuce, leaves separated"
- "0.5 cup queso fresco crumbled (use feta for sharper tang or cotija if unavailable)"
instructions:
- "==="
- "1 Preheat oven to 290°F, low enough to warm without crisping. Wrap tortillas in foil, seal tightly."
- "2 Whisk together oil, cumin, turmeric in a small bowl. Notice the bright yellow tint from turmeric—oil will be aromatic with cumin’s earthiness."
- "3 Trim steak clean, removing large fat chunks. Salt and pepper thoroughly, pressing seasoning into meat."
- "4 For stovetop, preheat a heavy cast-iron skillet on high, wait until almost smoking—oil flashes. Sear steak 4-5 minutes each side for medium rare, watching for a well-browned crust with deep Maillard spots. On grill, set to medium heat, direct flames, 4 minutes per side. Listen for hissing when meat hits hot surface—the zucchini smell of crust forming."
- "5 Remove steak, tent loosely with foil, rest 7 minutes—critical step. Resting lets juices redistribute; skip it, and the steak bleeds out like a cut wire."
- "6 Meanwhile, place foil-wrapped tortillas in oven for 8 minutes. Too long and they dry out; watch edges for softness."
- "7 Remove tortillas carefully, unwrap just one side, brush oil-spice mixture on that side. This seals flavor, adds a subtle greasy sheen keeping tortilla supple."
- "8 Slice steak thin against grain into roughly ⅜ inch strips—thinner slices better for layers, chew reduction."
- "9 Start assembly: lay a Bibb leaf over the basted tortilla side to form a bed. Add 2-3 slices beef, dollop a tablespoon of salsa, pile a pinch of shredded cabbage, sprinkle queso fresco over top."
- "10 Fold gently. Serve immediately to preserve textures. Optional squeeze of lime for extra zip."
