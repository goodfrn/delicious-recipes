ingredients:
- "10 tablespoons unsalted butter"
- "3/4 cup packed light brown sugar"
- "1 teaspoon almond extract"
- "1/3 cup sliced almonds"
- "1/4 teaspoon kosher salt"
- "About 12 whole graham crackers, broken in halves and quarters to fill pan"
instructions:
- "===Prepping the base==="
- "Heat oven to 405°F. Line a 10x15 jelly roll pan with heavy-duty foil; oil with nonstick spray. Layout graham cracker halves and quarters tightly covering every inch. It doesn't have to be perfect; overlap slightly if needed to avoid gaps."
- "===Making the caramel==="
- "In medium saucepan over medium-high, melt butter with brown sugar. Stir constantly. Look for tiny bubbles forming along edges. Once bubbling just begins, start a rolling boil for 3 1/2 minutes exactly, stirring occasionally to prevent scorching—too long, turns bitter; too short, won't set."
- "Remove pan from heat. Stir in almond extract, sliced almonds, and kosher salt quickly. Almonds will sizzle slightly; that's good—nut toast underway. Work fast."
- "===Assembly and baking==="
- "Pour caramel evenly over crackers—hot and runny but thickening. Use a spatula to spread gently without cracking cracker base. Try to cover all but some gaps add texture contrast."
- "Slide pan into oven for around 7 minutes or until caramel is bubbly, nuts toasted to golden brown, and edges of crackers caramelize dark amber. Watch carefully; sugar burns fast. Smell changes from sweet to nutty-honeyed, caramel surface hardens a bit but still tacky when poked."
- "===Cooling and finishing==="
- "Remove from oven; cool completely—no impatience here. Pan should feel warm before breaking. If caramel sticks to foil, slide knife edges gently underneath to free pieces without shattering."
- "Once cool, break into shards. Store airtight at room temp or refrigerate to keep nuts crisp. Reheat a few seconds to soften if too hard."
- "===Kitchen tips==="
- "Butter sub: margarine or coconut oil works but watch caramel firmness. Brown sugar swap: dark molasses sugar adds depth but shortens cook time. Almonds can be replaced by chopped walnuts or hazelnuts, adjust roast time accordingly."
- "If caramel hardens too fast while pouring, warm pan slightly or pour in halves in quick succession."
- "Never skip lining pan with foil; caramel sticks fiercely. Use a sturdy pan to distribute heat evenly; thin pans burn sugar corners."
- "Graham crackers can break unevenly; press down gently after placing for flat base."
- "Oven temps vary: trust bubbles and nut toasting, not just clock."
- "Avoid stirring caramel after boiling stage to prevent crystallization; just fold in nuts and salt off heat."
- "Salt is key for balancing sweetness, don’t omit even if mild. Sprinkle extra lightly on top before baking if you like a salted crust."
