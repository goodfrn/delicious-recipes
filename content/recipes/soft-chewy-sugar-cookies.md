ingredients:
- "2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour"
- "1/4 cup tapioca starch"
- "1 teaspoon baking powder"
- "1/2 teaspoon salt"
- "1/2 cup granulated sugar (reserved)"
- "1 cup unsalted butter softened"
- "3/4 cup light brown sugar packed"
- "3/4 cup granulated sugar"
- "1 large egg"
- "1 teaspoon vanilla extract"
- "1 teaspoon orange zest"
instructions:
- "Whisk flour, tapioca starch, baking powder, salt in medium bowl; set aside."
- "Put 1/2 cup granulated sugar in small bowl; reserve for rolling."
- "In large mixing bowl, beat butter with granulated and brown sugar on medium-low 1 1/2 minutes; creamy texture, some grit gone."
- "Add egg, vanilla, orange zest; beat another minute or until light, fluffy, pale yellow."
- "Slowly mix dry ingredients in low speed till absorbed; scrape bowl sides; dough stays soft but holds shape."
- "Use 1 1/2 tablespoon scoop, roll balls in reserved sugar evenly; tray ready."
- "Cover tray tightly; refrigerate 45 minutes minimum. Chilling stops spread; skipping leads to thin flat cookies."
- "Preheat oven 340 degrees Fahrenheit; line baking sheet with parchment."
- "Place 7-8 chilled dough balls spaced. Bake 9-11 minutes till edges faintly golden; centers should look set but soft."
- "Let cool on sheet 4-5 minutes to firm; transfer carefully to rack."
- "Repeat baking with remaining dough."
