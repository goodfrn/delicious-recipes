ingredients:
- "1 cup butterscotch chips"
- "1/2 cup creamy almond butter"
- "5 cups Rice Krispies cereal"
- "1/2 cup malted milk balls"
- "Parchment paper for lining"
instructions:
- "===Melt and Blend==="
- "In a medium saucepan set to low heat, melt butterscotch chips with almond butter. Stir constantly—look for a shiny, smooth liquid without streaks. If mixture looks grainy or separates, remove immediately. Heating gently avoids crystallization and burnt flavor."
- "===Prep Crunch Base==="
- "Pour Rice Krispies and malted milk balls into a large bowl. Use bigger bowl than expected; you'll need room to mix without smashing cereal."
- "===Combine and Coat==="
- "Pour melted butterscotch-almond butter mix over cereals. Toss gently but thoroughly. Break up clumps now to prevent uneven coating. Occasionally scrape sides to include every bit of sauce."
- "===Shape Cookies==="
- "Using a spoon, drop rounded mounds about two inches wide onto parchment. Leave space to avoid sticking. Wet spoon for cleaner scoops."
- "===Chill and Set==="
- "Place tray in fridge. Firmness depends on room temperature; if cookies feel sticky after 30 minutes, leave longer. Ready when firm to touch, no wetness underneath."
- "===Store==="
- "Keep in airtight container chilled or at room temperature up to a week. Warmer temps may cause softening. If too soft, refrigerate before serving."
