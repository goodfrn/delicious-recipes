ingredients:
- "1 box yellow cake mix"
- "1 package lemon gelatin mix (replaces pudding powder)"
- "1 1/4 cups water"
- "1/3 cup melted coconut oil (instead of vegetable oil)"
- "3 large eggs"
- "1 package lemon pudding mix"
- "1 1/2 cups cold milk"
- "1/4 cup powdered sugar plus extra to taste (for tartness balance)"
instructions:
- "Preheat oven to 345°F, rack middle position for even heat"
- "Combine cake mix and lemon gelatin powder in large bowl; dry mix with coarse whisk"
- "Add in water, melted coconut oil, eggs; stir just until ingredients blend, no overmixing—stop when no dry spots visible"
- "Pour batter into greased 9×13 pan; level lightly with spatula"
- "Slide pan onto middle rack; bake 23-28 minutes, watch for golden edges and toothpick with moist crumbs"
- "Remove cake, set on wire rack; wait 10 minutes until warm but firm to touch"
- "Poke 12 holes evenly across surface using handle of wooden spoon; holes about 1 inch wide"
- "Mix lemon pudding, cold milk, and powdered sugar; stir gently until it thickens slightly but still pourable"
- "Pour half the mixture slowly over cake, letting it seep into holes and crumb below"
- "Refrigerate cake 30 minutes while remaining pudding thickens to spreadable frosting consistency"
- "After pudding thickens, slather evenly over top of cake; cover loosely and chill 3-4 hours until fully set and firm"
- "Serve chilled; slice clean with serrated knife dipped in hot water if sticky"
