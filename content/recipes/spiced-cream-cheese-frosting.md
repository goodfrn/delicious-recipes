ingredients:
- "8 oz softened cream cheese"
- "6 oz softened unsalted butter"
- "2 tbsp heavy cream"
- "1 tbsp pure maple syrup"
- "1 tsp ground cardamom"
- "4 cups powdered sugar sifted"
instructions:
- "1. Use stand mixer with whisk or handheld on medium-high. Toss softened cream cheese and butter in bowl. Mix 2½ to 3 minutes. Look for pale, light texture. Should hold soft peaks; no lumps. Keep scraping bowl sides. Overmixing fat crushes structure, so stop once fluffy but still stable."
- "2. Pour in heavy cream; add maple syrup and cardamom. Stir at medium speed until well blended. Watch streaks fade, aroma shifts—note cardamom sharpness warming the scent. Don't rush; incorporate fully before next step."
- "3. Drop speed to medium-low. Slowly add powdered sugar half a cup at a time. Pause, mix well after each addition. Texture thickens, then smoothens. Stop when frosting is creamy, spreads easily but not runny. If stiff, splash extra cream, mix; too loose, add more sugar gradually."
- "4. Final check: scoop frosting up with spatula—should be dense enough to pipe, soft enough to spread cleanly. If grainy, mix longer or sieve sugar prior. Use immediately or refrigerate covered. Bring to room temp before use—remixing briefly brings texture back."
