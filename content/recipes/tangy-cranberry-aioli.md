ingredients:
- "0.7 cup cranberry orange relish"
- "0.4 cup garlic aioli"
- "1 teaspoon finely grated orange zest"
- "1/4 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves, chopped"
- "pinch salt"
- "pinch black pepper"
instructions:
- "=== Combine ingredients ==="
- "In a medium bowl, add cranberry orange relish and garlic aioli. Use a whisk or spoon; fold and stir until mixture is uniform with no visible streaks of either component. Look for a smooth, tinted pink consistency — not watery or separated."
- "=== Season and check texture ==="
- "Add orange zest and fresh thyme. Stir gently to distribute small flecks evenly throughout. Season with salt and black pepper to bring out the herbal and tangy notes. Taste. Adjust salt slightly if cranberry is bland or overly tart. Texture should be creamy but hold shape; if too thick, a teaspoon of olive oil or lemon juice thinned in helps."
- "=== Rest and meld flavors ==="
- "Transfer to airtight container. Refrigerate for 25-35 minutes. This rest is short but essential — herbs release aroma, flavors meld. Avoid chilling longer than 1 hour or it may lose brightness, becoming muted and denser."
- "=== Use and store ==="
- "Serve chilled or near room temp for best aroma lift. Holds in fridge 2-3 days. Stir before serving if settling or liquid separation occurs. Substitute: swap garlic aioli for plain mayo, add minced garlic separately; or use Greek yogurt for lighter version but expect less richness."
