ingredients:
- "8oz cream cheese softened"
- "1 cup sour cream"
- "1 cup sharp cheddar shredded"
- "4 slices pancetta chopped crisp"
- "1 small jalapeño finely diced"
- "1/4 tsp garlic powder"
- "1/4 tsp smoked paprika"
- "Fresh cracked black pepper to taste"
- "Assortment crackers and vegetable sticks for serving"
instructions:
- "Preheat oven to 375F. Butter or grease shallow pie dish or oven-safe small baking dish."
- "In mixing bowl, soften cream cheese with spoon or mixer until creamy but still thick. Avoid long beating or it will thin out."
- "Fold in sour cream and shredded cheddar gradually. Mix just until homogenous. Over mixing can break texture."
- "Add jalapeño, garlic powder, smoked paprika, pepper. Stir to distribute evenly. Taste to confirm spice balance."
- "Transfer mixture to prepared dish, spreading flat and even. Do not overfill, leave 1/4 inch gap from rim."
- "Top with all crispy pancetta pieces evenly. Penny-sized bits melt into the cheese pool and crisp on top."
- "Put in oven. Listen for slow bubble forming along edges after about 20 minutes. Edges should just start to brown; center feels set but slightly jiggly."
- "If top isn’t crisp enough after 25 minutes, switch to broil for 1-2 minutes, watch carefully to prevent burning."
- "Remove and let cool 5 minutes to thicken slightly. Serve warm with crackers and raw vegetables to balance richness."
