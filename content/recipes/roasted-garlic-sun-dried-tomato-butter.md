ingredients:
- "1 cup unsalted butter room temp"
- "4 cloves roasted garlic mashed smooth"
- "1/3 cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes not oily; rehydrated in warm water 10 mins if needed"
- "2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh parsley"
- "1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil or thyme optional twist"
- "1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika optional for subtle smoky note"
- "Pinch salt adjust to taste"
instructions:
- "Chop roasted garlic cloves finely; mash with a fork until creamy but not watery."
- "Attach whisk to mixer. Whip butter on medium-high speed until it looks lighter in color and fluffy texture forms about 1 1/2 minutes. Scrape bowl sides with spatula midway to incorporate uniformly."
- "Add mashed garlic to whipped butter; mix on low speed for 45 seconds until fully incorporated. Avoid over-whipping or butter may start softening too much."
- "Fold sun-dried tomatoes and parsley plus optional herbs into whipped butter with spatula. Use gentle strokes to keep texture intact; do not overwork. Sprinkle smoked paprika and salt during folding."
- "Transfer compound butter to airtight container or shape into log on parchment. Refrigerate minimum 20 minutes to firm up; can store up to 3 days. Let soften at room temperature 10 mins before using to reveal full spreadability and flavor."
- "If tomatoes too dry, soak in warm water 10 minutes, drain and pat dry before folding. Garlic should smell mellow, not sharp raw. Butter texture key: fluffy but firm enough to hold shape, not melting or overly soft."
