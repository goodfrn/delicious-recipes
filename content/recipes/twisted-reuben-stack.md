ingredients:
- "6 tablespoons chipotle mayonnaise"
- "2 tablespoons ketchup"
- "¼ teaspoon kosher salt"
- "¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper"
- "1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce"
- "½ teaspoon lemon juice"
- "8 slices rye bread"
- "3 tablespoons mustard oil or softened butter"
- "8 slices Swiss cheese"
- "12 ounces sliced corned beef or extra lean smoked brisket"
- "½ cup quick-pickled cabbage (substitute for sauerkraut: thin cabbage strips, 1 cup vinegar, 1 teaspoon sugar, pinch salt, soak 20 minutes)"
- "1 teaspoon prepared horseradish sauce"
instructions:
- "=== Quick-pickle cabbage === Mix cabbage strips, vinegar, sugar, salt in bowl. Press down, set aside while prepping other parts. Drains excess liquid later if watery."
- "=== Season the sauce === Whisk chipotle mayo with ketchup, Worcestershire, lemon juice, horseradish sauce; salt and pepper fold in at end. Spicy, tangy, smoky notes layered in. Taste and adjust salt carefully; smoking mayo boosts flavor."
- "=== Prep bread and layer === Spread mustard oil or butter on one side of each bread slice. Opposite side, spread a thin coat of sauce. Butter side out, sauce side in—crisp outside, moist inside. Avoid sogginess by keeping sauce thin and bread firm."
- "Layer for each: bread slice (sauce side up), one Swiss cheese slice, corned beef slices clustered evenly, ¼ cup quick-pickled cabbage drained, second slice Swiss cheese, top with bread (sauce side down). Cheese seals cabbage moisture. Press lightly."
- "=== Heat skillet === Medium-low heat, non-stick pan preferred. Place sandwiches in skillet, don’t overcrowd. Hear gentle sizzle, watch edges brown but move quickly if starts burning. Lower heat early if browning uneven or too fast."
- "=== Flip carefully === Use wide spatula. Cheese melts make flipping tricky. Flip once first side shows deep golden brown, about 8-10 minutes. Press sandwich gently with spatula to compact layers for better melt and hold."
- "Cook second side until same golden crust, cheese fully melted inside—check by slight give pressing bread. Total cook about 20-25 minutes. Avoid high heat to prevent burnt bread with cold insides."
- "=== Rest briefly === Sandwich off heat, 2 minutes letting cheese set slightly. Cuts cleaner, flavors meld better."
- "Serve warm. If sauce drips or soggy happens, next time squeeze excess pickle liquid out better or toast bread dry briefly before buttering."
