ingredients:
- "115g unsalted butter softened"
- "100g light brown sugar packed"
- "1 large egg room temp"
- "1 tsp pure vanilla extract"
- "120g all-purpose flour sifted"
- "20g Dutch-processed cocoa powder sifted"
- "1/2 tsp baking soda"
- "Pinch salt"
- "Ice cream 1 pint (any flavor preferred)"
- "Optional: crushed toasted pecans or rainbow sprinkles"
instructions:
- "Heat oven to 335°F. Don’t trust oven gauge blindly. Use visual cues."
- "Beat butter and sugar together vigorously. Look for light color and texture breaking down granules. Around 3 minutes but don’t race it."
- "Scrape sides meticulously. Add egg and vanilla. Mix just to combine. Over-beating here makes toughness."
- "Sift flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt. Integrate dry into wet gently. Dry lumps? No. No overmix. Dough firm but pliable. Divide in two equal parts."
- "Between parchment sheets, roll dough till roughly 1/8 inch thick. Thicker means cakier cookie, thinner risks breaking. Watch edges for evenness."
- "Use 3-inch wide jar or cutter to punch rounds. Fork poke 6-8 holes evenly per cookie. Prevents bubbles and uneven rise."
- "Arrange on lined sheet pan spaced. Bake 13–16 minutes max. Homes vary. Tops lose gloss and slightly cracked edges signal done. Undercooked: shiny, too soft to handle."
- "Cool completely on wire rack. Too warm? Ice cream melts and sandwiches lose shape."
- "Ice cream out for 5 minutes. Still cold but soft enough to slice with serrated knife. Cut block into quarters with container on. Peel paper off immediately."
- "Sandwich ice cream between two cookies, pressing slightly for adhesion. Roll edges in pecans or sprinkles if desired."
- "Wrap individually in plastic wrap or freezer paper. Freeze at least 2 hours before serving. This firms edges and seals texture."
- "Common issues: dough too sticky? Chill 15 minutes. Cookies cracked? Dough too dry or rolled unevenly. Ice cream too hard to cut? Let warm 3-5 min but not melted."
- "Substitute brown sugar with coconut sugar for deeper flavor. Use almond flour for lighter texture but alter baking time by a few minutes. Vanilla can swap to almond extract for twist."
- "Mind freezer temps — fluctuations cause ice crystals. Wrap well to avoid freezer burn."
- "Keep knife warm under hot water and dry before slicing ice cream. Prevents cracking."
