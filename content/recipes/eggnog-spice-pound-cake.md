ingredients:
- "2 cups all-purpose flour"
- "1 teaspoon baking powder"
- "1/4 teaspoon salt"
- "1 teaspoon ground nutmeg"
- "1/2 teaspoon ground ginger"
- "1 cup granulated sugar"
- "3/4 cup coconut oil softened"
- "4 large eggs"
- "1 teaspoon almond extract"
- "3/4 cup eggnog"
- "2 tablespoons dark rum"
- "for glaze==="
- "1 cup powdered sugar"
- "2 tablespoons maple syrup"
instructions:
- "Grease 9x5 inch loaf pan with shortening or butter. Flour pan lightly but thoroughly—avoids sticking. Keep it tight; no skipping this step or whole cake wrecked."
- "Preheat oven to 320 degrees Fahrenheit. Lower temp a tad; pound cakes better slow-cooked. Golden crust forms without burning. Clean edges pulling from pan signal near ready, not oven clock alone."
- "Mix flour, baking powder, salt, nutmeg, and ginger in bowl. Whisk well to distribute spices evenly. Don’t rush here. Uneven spice means blotchy flavor."
- "In large bowl, beat coconut oil and sugar until pale and fluffy. Takes 3-4 minutes at medium speed. Don't underbeat; sugar needs time to dissolve and create airy texture."
- "Add eggs one at a time. Full incorporation key. After each egg, scrape sides. Incomplete mixing leaves lumps or oily streaks that ruin crumb."
- "Blend in almond extract and rum—adds complexity beyond vanilla. Stir carefully; keep batter silky, not overworked."
- "Alternately add dry ingredients and eggnog. Start with flour mixture and finish with flour. Mix gently after each addition. Overmixing toughens cake; under mixing leaves flour pockets."
- "Pour batter into pan; stop filling at 3/4 level max. Too full means batter spills over, dry edges, or dense middle."
- "Bake for approx 60 to 70 minutes. Begin checking at 55 minutes. Look for golden brown crust, fragrant spices filling room, and edges pulling clean from pan sides."
- "Insert toothpick in thickest part; clean or moist crumbs cling okay. Wet batter means more time needed."
- "Cool cake 15 minutes in pan to set structure. Too soon release? Cake breaks, soggy wreck."
- "Invert onto cooling rack gently. Let fully cool before glazing. Warm cake melts glaze, turns messy."
- "For glaze, mix powdered sugar and maple syrup until smooth. Consistency like thick honey. Drizzle slowly over cooled cake, letting sticky glaze pool and firm on edges."
- "Store covered at room temp max 2 days. Can freeze wrapped tightly for up to 1 month. Defrost wrapped to keep moisture."
- "Common issues: greasy spots? Undercreamed oil and sugar. Dense cake? Overmixing or old leavening."
- "Substitutions: use melted butter for coconut oil; lose some tropical aroma but keep richness. Rum optional; add more extract instead."
- "Replacing loaf with Bundt pan? Increase bake time by 15 minutes. Watch internal cues, not just timer."
