ingredients:
- "1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour"
- "2 tsp baking powder"
- "1/2 tsp salt"
- "3/4 cup unsalted butter softened"
- "1 cup granulated sugar"
- "1 tsp vanilla extract"
- "3 large eggs"
- "1 cup buttermilk room temperature"
- "1/3 cup blue and pink sprinkles mixed"
- "8 oz cream cheese softened"
- "3 cups powdered sugar sifted"
- "1/4 tsp salt"
- "1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract"
- "3 tbsp whole milk room temperature"
- "Blue gel food coloring small amount"
- "Pink gel food coloring small amount"
- "12 cupcake liners"
- "Optional sprinkle topping"
instructions:
- "=== Cupcakes ==="
- "Preheat oven to 355F. Grease cupcake pan lightly or use liners properly. Mix flour, baking powder, salt in small bowl. Whisk briskly – aerate dry mix before folding."
- "In large bowl, cream softened butter and sugar until pale color and fluffy texture. About 3 minutes with electric mixer. Add vanilla extract next."
- "Add eggs one at a time. Mix thoroughly after each addition. Avoid overmixing to keep batter light."
- "Add dry ingredients in thirds, alternating with buttermilk in halves. Start and end with dry mix. Fold gently—stop once no streaks remain. Overmixing kills volume."
- "Fold in sprinkles carefully. Avoid crushing sprinkles; fold with spatula for even distribution without color bleed."
- "Fill liners about two-thirds full. Don’t overfill — cupcakes will spill and flatten. Tap pan lightly to release air bubbles."
- "Bake 27-30 minutes. Use toothpick in center after 25 minutes. Should come out clean with few moist crumbs but no wet batter."
- "Cool completely in pan on wire rack before icing."
- "=== Icing and Assembly ==="
- "Beat cream cheese and butter until very smooth. Add powdered sugar and salt gradually at low speed to prevent sugar puffing. Beat 3-4 minutes on medium-high until thick and creamy."
- "Add vanilla and milk. Beat for another 90 seconds. Adjust thickness by adding milk teaspoon by teaspoon or powdered sugar to stiffen."
- "Separate ½ cup of plain icing. Tint half with blue gel, other half with pink gel to desired brightness."
- "Keep cupcakes in pan. Using Wilton 1A tip or small round cutter, hollow out the center of each cupcake about 1 inch deep. Keep cut tops intact—set aside."
- "Pipe colored icing into the hollow space carefully. Fill flush with cupcake surface. Replace the cut top piece, gently pressing down to seal."
- "Top entire cupcake with plain cream cheese icing. Use offset spatula or piping bag for smooth or decorated finish. Sprinkle decorations last."
- "Chill 15 minutes if icing feels soft—helps set filling and outer frosting."
- "Serve room temperature for best flavor and texture."
