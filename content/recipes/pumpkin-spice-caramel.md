ingredients:
- "3/4 cup maple syrup"
- "1/4 cup coconut sugar"
- "1/2 cup coconut cream"
- "2 tablespoons butter"
- "1 teaspoon vanilla bean paste"
- "2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice"
- "1/2 teaspoon espresso powder"
- "1/4 teaspoon baking soda"
instructions:
- "Combine maple syrup, coconut sugar, coconut cream, butter, vanilla bean paste, pumpkin pie spice, and espresso powder in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Whisk continuously to dissolve sugar and avoid scorching; watch closely as mixture heats."
- "Once small bubbles appear and steam rises, reduce heat to low. Maintain a gentle simmer. Within about 12-15 minutes, the syrup will thicken slightly — test by dipping a spoon and running your finger through the liquid coating. It should cling to the back of the spoon without dripping off immediately."
- "Remove from heat. Quickly whisk in baking soda. Expect immediate foaming; this lightens texture and creates a creamy mouthfeel. Stir until foam settles and syrup is uniform."
- "Allow syrup to cool to room temperature before using. Sauce thickens as it cools; if over-thick, gently rewarm with a splash of coconut cream or water, stirring to loosen. Store in airtight container refrigerated up to 5 days."
