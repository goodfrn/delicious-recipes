ingredients:
- "3 cups all purpose flour"
- "2 ½ teaspoons baking soda"
- "1 ½ teaspoons salt"
- "1 ¾ cups granulated sugar"
- "2 sticks unsalted butter softened (1 cup)"
- "3 large eggs room temperature"
- "1 tablespoon vanilla extract"
- "1 teaspoon almond extract"
- "1 cup Greek yogurt full fat"
- "1 cup whole milk"
- "½ cup buttermilk"
- "1 cup rainbow sprinkles"
- "=== Frosting ==="
- "8 ounces cream cheese softened"
- "½ cup unsalted butter softened"
- "4 cups powdered sugar sifted"
- "3-4 tablespoons heavy cream or milk"
- "1 teaspoon vanilla extract"
- "Pinch salt"
instructions:
- "Preheat oven to 350F. Grease a half sheet pan generously or line with parchment, then spray parchment and sides for foolproof release."
- "Mix dry ingredients in a bowl: flour, baking soda, salt. Set aside."
- "In stand mixer fitted with paddle, cream butter and sugar medium speed 3-4 minutes. Should be light, fluffy, pale. Scrape bowl sides often to catch any uncreamed butter."
- "Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each. Then dump in vanilla and almond extract. Blend until uniform."
- "Add Greek yogurt in two parts, scraping bowl after each addition."
- "Alternate adding dry mix and milk-buttermilk blend in thirds. Start and end with dry. Mix low speed till each addition just disappears. Avoid over mixing or tough crumb."
- "Gently fold sprinkles with spatula. Do not overwork or sprinkles will bleed color or sink."
- "Pour into prepared pan. Spread evenly, tap pan on counter to release air bubbles."
- "Bake 23-27 minutes. Look for golden edges pulling from pan, top dry to touch, and a toothpick with moist crumbs but no wet batter. Bounce test works: firm top that springs back means done."
- "Cool completely in pan set on wire rack. Any warmth ruins frosting consistency."
- "Frosting time. Beat cream cheese and butter medium speed till fluffy 2-3 minutes. Add powdered sugar gradually, alternating with cream. Scrape often. Add vanilla and salt last. Beat 3-4 minutes till light, creamy, pipeable. Adjust thickness with powdered sugar or cream. Avoid runny or gritty finishes."
- "Spread or pipe frosting as desired. Swirling with offset spatula creates rustic look. Piping perimeter looks sharp but needs steady hand."
- "Top with rainbow sprinkles for color and texture contrast."
- "For clean slices, heat knife with hot water and wipe between cuts to prevent drag and crumbs. Rest cake if too soft to slice."
- "Store layers covered at room temp or refrigerate for up to 3 days. Bring to room temp before serving for best texture."
