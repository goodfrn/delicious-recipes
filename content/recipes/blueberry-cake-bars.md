ingredients:
- "1 box yellow cake mix (approx 15.25 oz)"
- "3 large eggs"
- "3/4 cup unsalted butter, melted (or 3/4 cup vegetable oil)"
- "2 cups blueberry pie filling (can use homemade or store-bought)"
- "zest of 1 lemon"
- "1 cup powdered sugar"
- "2 oz cream cheese, softened"
- "1-2 tablespoons milk"
instructions:
- "Preheat oven to 355F. Grease or parchment-line a 9x13 pan. Butter gives richer crust, but oil works fine if need be."
- "In medium bowl, beat yellow cake mix, eggs, melted butter with hand mixer or stand. Mixture thick, crumbly, dough-like. If too dry, add splash milk."
- "Divide dough in half. Press one half firmly and evenly into the pan bottom, smooth with fingers or spatula, no bare spots. Pack it down; base must hold filling."
- "Bake base 18 minutes or until edges lightly browned, surface dry, not doughy or wet. Should smell buttery, toasted. Oven may vary, watch crust color."
- "Meanwhile, in small bowl, gently fold blueberry pie filling with lemon zest. Adds brightness, cuts sweetness, lifts aroma."
- "Spread blueberry mixture evenly over hot crust. Filling should sit slightly thick, not runny; if homemade or too watery, strain excess juice first."
- "Break reserved dough into uneven clumps, scatter over filling roughly. Leave some gaps, no flat layer. Creates texture, crunchy bits contrast soft fruit."
- "Return pan to oven. Bake another 15-20 minutes until top dough spots golden and filling edges bubble vigorously. Peek near crust edges for amber spots."
- "Remove bars. Cool completely in pan at room temp. Cooling sets filling, prevents cutting mess."
- "Whisk powdered sugar with softened cream cheese and 1 tablespoon milk for glaze; add milk gradually to reach thick but drizzly consistency."
- "Drizzle glaze zigzag over cooled bars. Let set five minutes, then slice into 24 squares with sharp knife, cleaning blade between cuts."
- "Serve at room temperature. Bars store airtight up to 3 days; excessive moisture from filling may soften crust overnight."
- "If crust soggy next day, toast individual bars in toaster oven at 350F for 5 minutes to crisp back up."
