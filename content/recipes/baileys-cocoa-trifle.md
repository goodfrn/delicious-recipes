ingredients:
- "8 oz cream cheese softened"
- "2 tbsp powdered sugar plus 3 tbsp separate"
- "1 ½ tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder divided"
- "1 ½ tsp vanilla extract divided"
- "3 tbsp Irish cream liqueur like Baileys or Kahlua plus 1 tbsp optional for cream"
- "1 cup heavy whipping cream chilled"
- "10 Oreo cookies or substitute chocolate wafer cookies"
- "Pinch fine sea salt"
instructions:
- "Crush cookies by sealing in medium plastic bag. Use rolling pin with even weight, tap gently until fine but not powdery crumbs form. Set aside."
- "In medium bowl, beat softened cream cheese with 2 tablespoons powdered sugar, 1 tablespoon cocoa powder, 1 teaspoon vanilla, and salt. Blend till combined but watch for grainy spots; scrape sides often. Add 3 tablespoons Irish cream and mix till fluffy. Proper texture means light, not runny."
- "Chill bowl and beaters for heavy cream if possible. Beat 1 cup cold heavy cream until volume doubles, starts to thicken but not stiff yet. Add 1 tablespoon Irish cream, 1 teaspoon vanilla, remaining cocoa powder, and 3 tablespoons powdered sugar. Continue beating until stiff peaks that hold shape but still glossy form. Avoid overbeating -- grainy, butter starts to separate."
- "Transfer cheesecake mixture into piping bag without tip. Another piping bag, fitted with open star tip (#1M or similar), fills with whipped cream mixture for final decoration."
- "Assemble. Lowball glasses or clear small jars work best for layers and visuals. Begin with thin layer crushed cookie crumbs for base. Pipe or spoon cheesecake mixture over crumb layer evenly. Follow with whipped cream -- pipe a little or spread gently to keep layers defined."
- "Repeat cookie crumbs then cheesecake layer again. Finish top with whipped cream swirl using star tip. Sprinkle last cookie crumbs lightly on top to add texture and visual contrast."
- "Chill assembled trifles minimum 15 minutes for flavors to meld and stabilize. Serve cold. If stored longer than 2 hours, cookie crumbs soften but remain tasty. Alternative: use gluten free or nut-based cookies for textural twist."
- "Common misstep: Adding Baileys too early to cream cheese mix dilutes and thins; add after base components mix. Another: overbeating whipped cream causes graininess, lose that glossy sheen and texture."
