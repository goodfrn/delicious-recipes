ingredients:
- "2 tablespoons olive oil"
- "1 cup creamed corn, drained"
- "1 1/4 cups cornbread mix (reduce original cornmeal and flour by 30%)"
- "2 large eggs"
- "3/4 cup full-fat Greek yogurt"
- "4 tablespoons browned butter"
- "1 teaspoon baking powder"
- "1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika"
- "1/2 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese"
instructions:
- "=== Preheat and Oil ==="
- "Place olive oil into a 12-inch cast iron skillet. Set oven to 430 degrees Fahrenheit. Put skillet with oil into the oven while it warms. Hot oil means instant crust. Don't skip this or cornbread sticks or steams."
- "=== Batter Preparation ==="
- "In a large bowl, combine cornbread mix, baking powder, smoked paprika, and sharp cheddar cheese. Mix dry ingredients lightly to distribute. Add eggs, Greek yogurt, creamed corn, and browned butter. Fold gently with a spatula. Overmixing ruins crumb. Batter thick but still pourable."
- "=== Baking ==="
- "Pull out the skillet once oven shows full temp. Hot oil will shimmer if ready. Pour batter carefully in skillet. Beware hot oil splash—use oven mitts like a champ. Swirl to even out."
- "Slide skillet back into oven. Watch edges: they turn golden, beginning to pull away from pan walls. Sound changes too—edges crisp, crackling faintly. Around 30 minutes total, but rely on sight and touch."
- "Insert toothpick near center; it should come out with moist crumbs, not wet batter. If still gummy, bake 3–5 minutes more."
- "=== Serving ==="
- "Cool slightly in skillet to firm up. Warm cornbread releases buttery aroma, slightly smoky note from paprika. Crust crisp; inside tender with cheese pull. Slice with a serrated knife to keep texture."
- "Leftovers reheat in a toaster oven to revive crisp edges. Microwave makes it soggy, avoid if possible."
