ingredients:
- "4 cups shredded broccoli slaw mix"
- "½ cup creamy dressing made with ¼ cup finely chopped walnuts, 2 tbsp maple syrup, 2 tbsp mayonnaise, 1 tsp apple cider vinegar"
- "⅓ cup toasted sunflower seeds"
- "¾ teaspoon kosher salt"
instructions:
- "Toast seeds in dry pan over medium heat until barely golden and nutty aroma rises, about 2-3 minutes, shaking frequently to avoid burning."
- "Combine broccoli slaw and toasted sunflower seeds in medium bowl."
- "Whisk together walnuts blended finely with maple syrup, mayo, and apple cider vinegar until creamy; taste for balance between sweet, tang, and richness."
- "Add dressing and kosher salt to slaw mixture; toss vigorously so every shred has coating - no clumps, no drowning."
- "Cover bowl with plastic wrap or towel. Let sit on counter for 12-17 minutes. Listen for slight condensation forming inside bowl, watch slaw slightly soften but remain crisp."
- "Just before serving, toss slaw again to fluff up, reviving crunch and redistributing dressing evenly."
- "Store leftovers tightly covered in fridge up to 2 days. Dressing will separate slightly; toss before eating."
- "Substitution tips: Swap broccoli slaw for a mix of shredded cauliflower and kale for depth. Walnuts replaced with pecans or almonds. Seeds can be pepitas or sliced almonds toasted. Mayo may be Greek yogurt or vegan mayo based on preference."
