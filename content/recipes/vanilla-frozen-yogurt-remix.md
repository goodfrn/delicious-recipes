- "Add Greek yogurt, heavy cream, maple syrup, cinnamon, and salt to blender."
- "Pulse 20 seconds; aim for just combined. Overblending traps air, ruins texture."
- "Pour into CREAMi™ pint. Leave lid off — prevents ice dome that can damage blade during spin."
- "Freeze flat surface, 22 hours minimum. Thicker ice, better spin but watch for uneven freezing."
- "After freezing, slot pint in outer bowl, lock lid on bowl tightly."
- "Mount bowl assembly on motor base; twist handle right to raise platform and lock firmly."
- "Run frozen yogurt program. Listen for smooth hum. If mixture still icy, respin once, max."
- "Remove pint. Add mix-ins if desired. Eat immediately or store tightly sealed for short term."
- "Cleanup tip: soak plastic parts in warm water. Blade won’t tolerate leftover sugar crystals or ice shards—can dull fast."
