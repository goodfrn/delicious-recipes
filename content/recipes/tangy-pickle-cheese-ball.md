ingredients:
- "8 ounces softened cream cheese"
- "1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce"
- "2 tablespoons dill pickle juice"
- "1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika"
- "1/2 teaspoon onion powder"
- "1/4 teaspoon lemon pepper seasoning"
- "1/2 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese"
- "1/4 cup finely diced dill pickles"
- "2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives"
- "1 teaspoon hot sauce (optional)"
instructions:
- "Start by softening cream cheese to room temp. Use a microwave for 10-15 seconds if needed but avoid melting."
- "In a medium bowl, stir softened cream cheese, Worcestershire sauce, and pickle juice vigorously until creamy and smooth with no lumps."
- "Add smoked paprika, onion powder, and lemon pepper seasoning. Mix to evenly distribute spices. Smell is key here, should smell smoky and tangy."
- "Fold in sharp cheddar cheese, diced pickles, chopped fresh chives. Use a rubber spatula thoroughly folding, not stirring fast - avoid air pockets."
- "Optional: drizzle hot sauce in, fold in gently for subtle heat balance."
- "Line a medium bowl with plastic wrap, leave enough overhang. Press mixture into bowl firmly, gathering plastic wrap over top."
- "Twist plastic to compact the cheese into a tight ball, pushing out air gaps. Should hold shape with some spring back when pressed."
- "Refrigerate for 3.5 to 4 hours minimum. Texture firms up to hold shape but remains soft enough to spread smoothly on crackers."
- "Remove from refrigerator 10 minutes before serving to soften slightly. Unwrap ball, place on platter."
- "Serve with sturdy crackers or vegetable slices that hold up to spread. Avoid overly soft crackers that rupture easily."
