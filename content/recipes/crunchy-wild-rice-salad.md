ingredients:
- "1 cup wild rice blend"
- "2 tbsp toasted sesame oil"
- "1 1/2 tbsp soy sauce"
- "1 tbsp chili garlic sauce or sambal oelek as chili crunch substitute"
- "1/2 cup diced cucumber"
- "1/2 cup shredded carrot"
- "3 green onions sliced"
- "1/2 cup chopped celery"
- "1 small red bell pepper diced"
- "1/4 cup roughly chopped fresh cilantro"
- "2 tbsp olive oil"
- "2 tbsp rice vinegar"
- "1 tsp honey or maple syrup"
- "Salt to taste"
- "Fresh lime wedges for serving"
instructions:
- "=== Cook Rice ==="
- "Rinse wild rice blend under cold water, drain well. Simmer rice in salted water, loose lid, until kernels start to split and chew but not mushy. Drain and spread on parchment-covered sheet. Let cool to room temp, no steaming trapped or grains clump. Refrigerate 1-3 hours or overnight to firm up. Cold rice bakes better, holds shape."
- "=== Crisp Rice ==="
- "Heat oven to 405°F (about 205°C). Place chilled rice in mixing bowl. Drizzle with sesame oil, soy sauce, chili garlic sauce. Stir and fold carefully; rice should glisten, no clumps. Spread thin layer on parchment-lined baking sheet. Bake 18–25 minutes, toss halfway, listen for crackles, watch color transitions—golden flecks, slight blister. Avoid dark burn spots—stop early if edges get too firm, chew still perceptible. Remove, let cool fully to finish crisping. Texture here makes or breaks dish."
- "=== Prepare Dressing ==="
- "Combine olive oil, rice vinegar, honey, pinch salt in jar or bowl. Whisk or shake vigorously to emulsify. Taste immediately. Adjust tang (vinegar), sweetness (honey), or oil depending on acidity and balance. Cold dressing rounds sharp vinegar punch, integrate later. Refrigerate until salad assembly."
- "=== Chop Vegetables ==="
- "Dice cucumber small cubes, carrots thin matchsticks, celery medium pieces for crunch. Slice green onions thin on angle. Red bell pepper chopped rough, not too large to keep bites consistent. Toss in large bowl. Fresh herbs like cilantro add bright contrast but optional if unavailable."
- "=== Assemble Salad ==="
- "Add crispy wild rice to bowl of vegetables. Pour dressing over top sparingly; toss gently but thoroughly. More dressing can drown crisp rice—less is more. Adjust seasoning with salt or squeeze lime over for freshness and bite. Toss again."
- "=== Serve ==="
- "Plate immediately or refrigerate briefly for melded flavors but best slightly chilled. Garnish with extra green onions or cilantro. Serve with lime wedges for optional citrus brightness. Eat crisp, textural contrast front and center."
