ingredients:
- "4 boneless skinless chicken breasts"
- "4 ounces softened cream cheese"
- "2 teaspoons minced garlic or garlic paste"
- "1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese"
- "1/3 cup grated parmesan cheese"
- "1 teaspoon black pepper"
- "1 teaspoon smoked paprika"
- "optional salt to taste"
instructions:
- "Preheat oven to 380 degrees Fahrenheit. Glass or ceramic baking dish works best to distribute heat evenly."
- "Pat chicken breasts dry with paper towels. Moisture on surface prevents proper browning and causes watery topping."
- "Mix softened cream cheese, minced garlic, cheddar, and parmesan until homogeneous. Texture should be spreadable but firm to hold on chicken."
- "Scatter black pepper and smoked paprika over chicken, pressing lightly for adhesion. Skip salt if using salted cheeses."
- "Spoon and pat the cheese mixture onto chicken breasts evenly, coat fully. Thick layer protects meat and traps juices inside."
- "Place dish in oven center rack. Bake 38 to 43 minutes watching cheese bubble and brown spots appear. Chicken done when internal temp hits 165°F or juices run clear."
- "After baking, let rest 5 minutes uncovered. Resting locks in moisture and finishes cooking gently. Cheese will set slightly to hold shape when sliced."
