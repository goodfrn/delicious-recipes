ingredients:
- "250g ginger snap cookies , finely ground"
- "110g unsalted butter , melted"
- "360ml heavy whipping cream , cold"
- "450g cream cheese , softened"
- "90g powdered sugar"
- "130g smooth almond butter"
- "120g sour cream"
- "1 tsp vanilla extract"
- "1/4 tsp fine sea salt"
- "140g smooth biscoff cookie spread"
- "Optional garnish , toasted sliced almonds , crushed ginger snap crumbs"
instructions:
- "Pulse ginger snaps in food processor until fine, almost sandy but no large chunks; texture crucial for crust binding."
- "Stream melted butter into crumbs while pulsing to combine evenly; mixture still sandy but sticks when pressed between fingers."
- "Line two 12-cup muffin pans with cupcake liners or lightly spray two mini cheesecake pans; scoop about 1 tbsp crust into each liner and firmly press down to compact crust layer."
- "Set pan in fridge or freezer to firm crust while prepping filling; chilling sets butter and prevents soggy base."
- "Chill mixing bowl and beaters ahead to whip cream faster; whip cold heavy cream on medium-high until stiff peaks form; quit before it goes grainy or buttery. Store covered in fridge."
- "In separate bowl, mix cream cheese, powdered sugar, almond butter, sour cream, vanilla, and salt at medium speed until completely smooth; no lumps means consistent richness."
- "Fold whipped cream in gently using rubber spatula; avoid overmixing to preserve aeration, or cheesecake will be dense and heavy."
- "Transfer filling to piping bag fitted with round 1A tip; pipe onto crust in even layers; fill liners fully but do not overfill to avoid spilling."
- "Refrigerate 5 to 6 hours (slightly shorter than usual) to let filling set and develop tangy flavor; cover loosely with plastic wrap to prevent skin forming."
- "Warm biscoff spread 20 seconds in microwave until fluid enough for spreading but not hot; smooth on top of chilled cheesecakes allowing natural drips to add rustic visual."
- "Return to fridge 10 to 15 minutes until spread is firm to touch; longer chilling yields firmer top but loses some sheen."
- "Sprinkle toasted sliced almonds and crushed ginger snaps on top for crunch and complementary spice notes; press lightly to stick."
- "Serve cold straight from fridge; texture should hold shape but yield under gentle pressure."
