ingredients:
- "1 package instant butterscotch pudding mix 3.4 oz"
- "2 cups unsweetened almond milk or preferred milk"
- "4 medium ripe bananas peeled and sliced thick"
- "1 tablespoon fresh lime juice"
- "1 1/2 cups whipped heavy cream, chilled"
- "1 cup mini marshmallows"
- "1 1/2 cups crushed graham crackers"
instructions:
- "=== Pudding Base ==="
- "1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together pudding mix and almond milk briskly. Look for a smooth, thickening batter within 2-3 minutes. It should coat the back of a spoon without running off quickly. If too thin, wait a couple minutes longer; too thick means overmixed or wrong liquid temp."
- "=== Bananas ==="
- "2. Slice bananas into 1/4 inch thick disks. Toss immediately with lime juice in a separate bowl. Lime juice is acidic enough to prevent browning but not overwhelm flavor. Get this done quick. Browning means oxidized flavor, unwanted mushiness later."
- "=== Fold Together ==="
- "3. Gently fold whipped cream into pudding. Folding, not stirring; use a rubber spatula with slow undercutting strokes to keep air in. You want a light texture but stable mousse-like consistency. No deflating or overworking."
- "4. Add bananas, mini marshmallows, crushed graham crackers into pudding-cream mix. Fold carefully just until combined. Marshmallows give chew and sweetness. Graham crackers provide crunch and a honeyed depth instead of vanilla wafers’ soft cookie flavor."
- "=== Serving & Storage ==="
- "5. Serve immediately to enjoy texture contrasts — creamy pudding, soft bananas, marshmallows, crunchy graham crackers. Or chill for 15-20 minutes if you want softer crackers. Longer chilling will cause crackers to sog and mush to lose zip. If refrigerated overnight expect thick pudding and very soft crackers; consider adding fresh crackers at serving."
- "=== Practical Tips ==="
- "If whipped cream is too soft, freeze bowl before whipping. Use fresh bananas, avoid underripe or overly ripe as too much moisture ruins texture. If no pudding mix, use homemade cornstarch custard base, chilling to cool before folding in cream."
- "For dairy-free, coconut whipped cream works but watch integration as it behaves differently, fold slower and more gently. Adjust lime juice to tolerance. Marshmallows can be toasted lightly under broiler for smoky depth before folding in."
- "Common mistake: overmixing pudding before thickened stage causes graininess or separation. Wait for pudding to visually thicken before folding in cream. Resist tasting early — uncooked pudding powder taste."
- "To speed prep, slice bananas last — keep in fridge until step 2. Use a wide spatula, large bowl to ease folding. Folding technique prevents loss of volume critical for fluffy mouthfeel."
