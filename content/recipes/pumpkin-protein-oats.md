ingredients:
- "1/2 cup canned sweet potato puree"
- "1/3 cup cottage cheese"
- "1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk"
- "1 cup rolled oats"
- "1 tablespoon chia seeds"
- "1 scoop vanilla whey protein powder"
- "1 teaspoon cinnamon"
- "1/2 teaspoon ground ginger"
- "1/4 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg"
- "1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract"
- "1 tablespoon maple syrup"
instructions:
- "Whisk sweet potato, cottage cheese, and almond milk vigorously in medium bowl. Look for smooth blend without lumps; cottage cheese texture varies so mix till consistent but don't overbeat."
- "Add oats, chia seeds, protein powder, cinnamon, ground ginger, nutmeg, vanilla extract, and maple syrup. Stir thoroughly to hydrate oats evenly. Get all dry ingredients coated; pockets of powder resist absorbing overnight if not stirred well."
- "Divide mixture evenly between two pint jars or containers with lids. Seal tight. Refrigerate at least 5 hours; ideally 7–8. Oats should swell, gel mixture thickens. Check texture by pressing surface lightly—springy but not watery. If too dense, add splash milk."
- "In the morning, add sliced toasted pecans, shredded coconut, or fresh berries. If oats feel too stiff, stir in a splash of additional almond milk or heat 30 seconds in microwave—it’s about clocking aroma and warmth. Microwave produces a faint steam scent signaling readiness, also softens oats quickly."
- "Eat directly from jars or transfer to bowl if preferred. Texture should balance chew and creaminess; notes of sweet spice vibrant."
- "Store leftovers up to 3 days refrigerated. Stir before consuming for best mouthfeel."
