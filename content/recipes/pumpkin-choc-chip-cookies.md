ingredients:
- "1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour"
- "1/2 teaspoon baking soda"
- "1/2 teaspoon fine salt"
- "1 teaspoon ground cinnamon"
- "1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg"
- "1/4 teaspoon ground ginger"
- "1 large egg"
- "1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract"
- "3/4 cup pumpkin puree"
- "6 tablespoons melted unsalted butter, cooled"
- "1/3 cup pure maple syrup"
- "1 cup dark chocolate chunks or chips"
instructions:
- "=== Dry Mix ==="
- "Whisk flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger in a medium bowl. Ensure no lumps."
- "=== Wet Mix ==="
- "In large bowl, beat egg lightly with vanilla, pumpkin puree, maple syrup, and cooled melted butter till homogenous. Avoid overmixing."
- "=== Combine ==="
- "Slowly stream dry ingredients into wet bowl. Fold gently to combine without overworking gluten. Dough sticky but manageable."
- "=== Chocolate ==="
- "Fold in chocolate chunks evenly. Reserve handful for topping."
- "=== Rest ==="
- "Chill or rest batter 25 minutes at room temp or fridge—hydrates flour and spices bloom."
- "=== Oven Prep ==="
- "Preheat oven to 355°F (180°C). Grease or line baking sheets with parchment."
- "=== Shaping ==="
- "Use two tablespoon-sized scoops. Drop onto sheet spaced comfortably apart. Lightly wet fingertips, gently pat tops to flatten slightly. Press 3-4 reserved chocolate chunks on top for visual appeal and extra melty spots."
- "=== Baking ==="
- "Bake 12 minutes. Look for edges turning golden and set, centers still soft but not wet. Centers firm up on cooling. Oven times vary; rely on visuals."
- "=== Cooling ==="
- "Let cookies rest on sheet 5 minutes post-bake to set. Then transfer to wire rack to cool fully."
