ingredients:
- "1 box Devil's Food Cake mix substitute 1 cup malted chocolate powder blended with dark cocoa powder and 1 1/4 cups evaporated milk"
- "3 large eggs"
- "1/2 cup vegetable oil"
- "2 cups whole milk or buttermilk alternative"
- "4.2 ounces instant vanilla pudding mix swapped for homemade vanilla pudding thickened with cornstarch and sugar"
- "14 sandwich Oreo cookies or similar chocolate cream sandwich cookies"
- "Butter or nonstick spray for pan"
instructions:
- "Preheat oven to 350F. Grease 9 by 13 pan with butter or spray, no paper liners - moisture traps and sog buildup can happen."
- "Mix dry cocoa malt blend with evaporated milk, oil, eggs vigorously. Don’t overmix or cake gets tough. Pour batter evenly."
- "Slide into oven. Cake done when edges pull slightly, center springs back pressed lightly, a skewer pops out with moist crumbs not batter."
- "Remove pan hot. Take a wooden spoon handle or thick straw and jab deep holes spaced 1-2 inches apart across entire surface. Do many so pudding settles deep, punch firmly to sound - sharp tap, not dull."
- "Cool cake until warm but not cold. Meanwhile whisk pudding mix into cold milk or homemade pudding into hot milk then cool until just thickened but still loose enough to pour."
- "Pour pudding over cake surface immediately before pudding sets in bowl, use spatula to nudge liquid into holes if needed. Tap pan on counter lightly to settle pudding."
- "Crush cookies placed in heavy zip bag or pulse 3-4 times in food processor. Not powder fine; should have some coarser bits for textural contrast."
- "Spread cookie crumbs evenly over pudding layer, press lightly to fix topping without compressing pudding below."
- "Cover loosely with foil, chill minimum 65 minutes or until pudding firms up but cake edges remain slightly yielding. Under chill = runny; over chill = pudding too stiff."
- "Serve chilled, slice with wet knife to avoid crumb drag. Extra cookies for garnish optional."
