ingredients:
- "4 tablespoons coconut oil softened"
- "5 cups Fruity Pebbles cereal"
- "12 ounces marshmallows"
- "zest of 1 lime optional"
- "pinch salt"
- "nonstick spray or additional coconut oil for pan"
instructions:
- "===Prepare Pan==="
- "Grab a 9x13 pan. Spread softened coconut oil on bottom and sides using your fingertips or half a paper towel. No skipping or scraping butter lumps that cause uneven sticking. Set aside."
- "===Melt & Mix==="
- "Heat large heavy-bottom pan over medium-low. Add marshmallows with coconut oil. Stir constantly—marshmallows puff, glossy, sticky, no char spots. Remove from heat once all melted and shiny. Immediate timing crucial here—don’t wait or mixture thickens or cools, makes mixing a nightmare."
- "Add Fruity Pebbles swiftly. Toss them in and fold gently but quickly; keep aerated with minimal smashing. Incorporate lime zest and a pinch salt for brightness pulling flavors forward. Texture: sticky yet colorful, marshmallows binding every pebble tightly but softly."
- "===Pack & Chill==="
- "Immediately transfer to the pan. Dampen fingers or a spatula with cool water; keeps mixture from stubborn sticking. Press flat, evenly packing down close but not crushing cereal. Pack too hard, treats dense and chewy, too loose, bars crumble. Visual cue: glossy marshmallow sheen dimming to matte means mix cooling, hardening."
- "Let sit untouched 25 to 35 minutes at room temperature. Fully cooled surface isn’t sticky, firm but pliable. Cut into squares with plastic knife or serrated blade—cleaner slice minimal crumbling. If bars feel tacky, chill 5 more minutes—over-chilling can dry and reduce chew. Enjoy crisp snap, tropical aftertaste, zest kick."
