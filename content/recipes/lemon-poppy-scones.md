ingredients:
- "1 ½ cups cold buttermilk"
- "1 tablespoon lemon zest"
- "8 tablespoons fresh lemon juice"
- "1 ½ cups all-purpose flour"
- "½ cup whole wheat flour"
- "3 tablespoons sugar"
- "1 tablespoon baking powder"
- "½ teaspoon coarse kosher salt"
- "1 tablespoon poppy seeds"
- "½ cup unsalted butter, grated"
- "2 tablespoons orange juice"
- "Glaze: 3 tablespoons powdered sugar"
- "Glaze: 2-3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice"
instructions:
- "Set oven 380℉. Heavy parchment on large sheet, no slip later."
- "Mix buttermilk, lemon zest, 6 tablespoons lemon juice, and orange juice in liquid cup. Stir convenience."
- "Sift or whisk flours, sugar, baking powder, salt, poppy seeds in big bowl. Break clumps, air it up. Baking powder spreads rise. Salt wakes flavors."
- "Butter grated fine. Cold important. Rub in quickly with fingers, pinch until pea-size bits, some bigger. Use too warm butter? Greasy sludge. Too hard? No mix. Balanced crumbles build flake."
- "Add wet mix to dry. Spoon or pastry cutter stir just to combine. Dough bulky but not sticky. Overmix toughens. Under-formed, won’t hold. Dimpled, uneven lumps okay."
- "Floured counter, turn dough out. Part to halves, patties about 1¼ inch thick. Thick enough essential to rise; thin collapses. No overhandling or warm hands."
- "Onto sheet, slice discs each crosswise into 6 triangles—wedge shape like pie. Leave gaps so air passes. Avoid crowding; soft sides are sad."
- "Bake 17-19 minutes. Edges golden, tops puffed, bottoms dry no bare spots. Tap top: firm with slight spring."
- "Cool on wire rack. Don’t skip or soggy forms underneath. Crust sets gently."
- "Glaze: Powdered sugar with 2 tablespoons lemon juice stirred until thick but pourable. Add last lemon juice bit slowly if dry or stiff."
- "Drizzle glaze over warm scones using spoon or fork tines. Cool further till glaze solidifies with crackled look. The bright tang cuts fat richness."
