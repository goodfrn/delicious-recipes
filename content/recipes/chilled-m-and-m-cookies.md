ingredients:
- "1 cup unsalted butter softened"
- "1 cup granulated sugar"
- "½ cup coconut sugar substitute for brown sugar adds caramel tone"
- "1 large egg"
- "1 teaspoon almond extract replace vanilla"
- "2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour sifted"
- "1 teaspoon baking soda"
- "½ teaspoon fine sea salt"
- "½ teaspoon ground cinnamon optional twist"
- "1 ½ cups M&Ms"
instructions:
- "Chill butter beforehand for better creaming texture. Preheat oven to 350F; rack center. Line baking sheet with parchment paper or silicone mat, no greasing necessary."
- "Cream butter with granulated and coconut sugars on medium speed till pale and airy, about 3-4 minutes. Key for soft, tender crumb. Incorporate egg, beat until smooth, no streaks. Add almond extract, mix briefly. Do not overmix or egg protein will toughen dough."
- "In large bowl, sift together flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon. Watch lumps—tiny clumps cause uneven rising. Slowly fold dry into wet using spatula. Stop mixing as soon as flour disappears, dough dense and thick, not sticky."
- "Fold in M&Ms by hand to prevent color bleed and bruising candies. Cover dough tightly with plastic wrap. Refrigerate minimum 1 ½ hours- firm dough spreads less, richer flavor develops."
- "Portion out 2 tablespoon balls using a cookie scoop or hands dusted lightly with flour. Roll gently into spheres roughly 2 inches diameter. Dough should feel like soft playdough—firm yet pliable. If too sticky, return to fridge 10 minutes."
- "Place balls at least 3 inches apart on sheet—cookies will spread and puff up. No flattening or pressing- forces gels to run and form thin edges instead of cakey centers."
- "Bake 12 to 14 minutes. Listen for quiet crackling as sugar caramelizes. Edges set and lightly browned but top stays shiny with slight cracks. Centers will look just set – soft but not raw. If cookie sinks on cooling, it was underbaked; if edges darken too much, reduce time."
- "After removing, cool cookies on sheet at least 4 minutes to firm up and finish baking. Then transfer gently to wire rack. Too hot or handling cookies prematurely breaks structure."
- "Store cooled cookies airtight room temp up to 4 days or freeze dough balls between parchment layers up to 1 month. Thaw chilled 15 minutes before baking."
- "Substitute semi-sweet chunks for some M&Ms for gooey pockets. Skip cinnamon if you want purely sweet profile. Avoid overbeating dough or too warm butter to limit spread. Dough always err on cooler side, give it time."
