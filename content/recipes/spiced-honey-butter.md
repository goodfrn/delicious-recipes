ingredients:
- "1 cup unsalted butter softened"
- "¾ cup brown sugar powdered"
- "2 tablespoons honey"
- "1 teaspoon ground cinnamon"
- "¼ teaspoon ground cardamom"
instructions:
- "Chop butter into chunks. Place in bowl. Start whipping with hand or stand mixer on medium speed."
- "Stop when butter lightens in color and fluff appears. Texture looks creamy not greasy — takes roughly 2 minutes but watch, feel."
- "Add powdered brown sugar, honey, cinnamon, cardamom."
- "Continue whipping. Scrape bowl edges a couple times to keep mix even. Look for uniform color, no sugar lumps — 2-3 minutes usually."
- "Taste quick. Sweetness should sing, spices warm but not sharp."
- "For tighter spread, cover with airtight lid, refrigerate one hour. Cold brings flavors together and firms texture."
- "If you skipped chill, use immediately on warm bread — melts in seconds, rich aroma curls up."
- "Store leftovers sealed in fridge, melts fast on counter — keep chilled and stir gently before reuse."
