ingredients:
- "4 oz cream cheese softened"
- "½ cup powdered sugar"
- "1 tbsp all-purpose flour"
- "1 tsp vanilla extract"
- "Pinch salt"
- "½ cup pumpkin puree blotted to ⅓ cup"
- "6 tbsp unsalted butter melted"
- "¾ cup packed brown sugar"
- "¼ cup granulated sugar"
- "1 large egg plus 1 yolk"
- "1 tsp vanilla extract"
- "1 ¾ cup all-purpose flour"
- "1 ½ tsp pumpkin pie spice"
- "½ tsp ground cinnamon"
- "½ tsp baking soda"
- "¼ tsp salt"
- "½ cup granulated sugar mixed with 1 tsp cinnamon or pumpkin pie spice"
instructions:
- "=== Cheesecake Filling ==="
- "Beat cream cheese with powdered sugar, 1 tbsp flour, vanilla, salt until mixture is fluffy, 2–4 minutes. Look for creamy texture without lumps. If cream cheese cold, soften longer or risk lumps."
- "Portion teaspoon-sized dollops onto parchment-lined tray. Freeze solid at least 90 minutes; can hold up to 72 hours sealed. Freeze well or filling runs and tanks cookies."
- "=== Prepare Pumpkin ==="
- "Spread tablespoonful pumpkin puree on plate. Blot with several paper towels until down to ⅓ cup. Moisture pulls gluten and thickens dough too much, makes cookies cakey. Dry pumpkin lasts longer too."
- "=== Wet Ingredients ==="
- "Whisk melted butter with brown sugar and granulated sugar until shiny and uniform. No grittiness. Butter must be cool but still liquid to avoid cooking egg too soon."
- "Add blotted pumpkin, whole egg, yolk, vanilla extract; beat until homogenous, glossy."
- "=== Dry Ingredients ==="
- "Whisk flour, pumpkin pie spice, cinnamon, baking soda, salt in separate bowl. Even distribution prevents random spice pockets or uneven rising."
- "=== Dough ==="
- "Fold dry mix gradually into wet using spatula or wooden spoon. Stir just enough to moisten ingredients; don’t beat. Overworking activates gluten, tough cookies. Stop when no streaks remain."
- "Cover dough, chill 30 minutes to 1 day. Chilling firms dough, controls spread, makes stuffing easier. Cold dough sticks less and holds shape better during bake."
- "=== Preheat Oven ==="
- "Set oven to 350°F (175°C). Prepare sheets with parchment to prevent sticking and browning bottoms."
- "=== Assembly ==="
- "Mix ½ cup sugar with cinnamon or pumpkin pie spice in small bowl."
- "Portion dough into 1.5–2 tbsp balls, about medium scoop size."
- "Flatten each ball into a disk, coat lightly with cinnamon sugar if sticky—it helps shaping."
- "Place frozen cheesecake ball in center. Wrap dough fully around, seal edges tightly. Roll into smooth spheres and toss in cinnamon sugar again for even coating."
- "=== Bake ==="
- "Arrange balls spaced 2 inches apart on sheets. Bake 9–12 minutes until edges faintly golden but centers still soft. Dough will continue to firm on sheet. Overbaking wastes softness and can dry filling."
- "=== Finishing Touch ==="
- "Immediately after oven, use round cutter or wide glass to 'scoot' cookies into perfect rounds while warm and flexible, about 30 seconds post-bake. Sets visually uniform cookies. Otherwise edges can dry irregularly."
- "=== Cooling ==="
- "Cool on sheets 2–3 minutes before transferring to racks. Filling sets as cookies cool but still moist and chewy."
- "Sprinkle with extra cinnamon sugar if desired once cool."
