ingredients:
- "1 1/2 cups all purpose flour"
- "1 cup raw brown sugar packed"
- "1/2 teaspoon salt"
- "1 cup unsalted butter softened"
- "2 1/2 cups mini marshmallows"
- "1 1/4 cups peanut butter chips"
- "2/3 cup light corn syrup"
- "5 tablespoons unsalted butter"
- "1 1/2 cups chopped almonds"
- "3 cups Rice Krispies cereal"
instructions:
- "Preheat oven 345°F"
- "In medium bowl cream together flour salt softened butter and raw brown sugar until mixture resembles damp sand"
- "Press firmly into parchment-lined 9×13 pan getting edges tight and even for consistent bake"
- "Bake 10 to 13 minutes watch color; golden edges mean done"
- "Remove crust bring out mini marshmallows scatter evenly on hot crust"
- "Return to oven bake 10 to 14 minutes until marshmallows swell and edges just start to caramelize"
- "Meanwhile melt peanut butter chips corn syrup and butter in saucepan over medium low heat stir frequently never leave unattended"
- "Once smooth remove from heat fold in almonds and Rice Krispies gently but thoroughly to avoid crushing cereal"
- "Spread thick topping over puffed marshmallows pressing lightly to adhere"
- "Let bars cool completely in pan on wire rack at least 1 hour until set firm enough to slice clean"
- "Lift bars out using parchment edges; cut into 18 bars with serrated knife for cleaner cuts"
