ingredients:
- "7 cups rice Chex cereal"
- "1 stick unsalted butter"
- "1 cup white chocolate chips"
- "½ cup almond butter"
- "1 tsp vanilla extract"
- "2 cups powdered sugar"
- "1 tsp turmeric powder or edible gold dust"
instructions:
- "Pick out a large bowl — must hold 7 cups cereal + coating, room for tossing; set aside."
- "Melt butter with white chips and almond butter over medium heat. Watch closely. Stir constantly to avoid scorching. Smooth, thick, and glossy signifies done."
- "Off heat, stir in vanilla quickly. Aroma should spike; that’s your signal."
- "Add cereal in 3 parts. Fold gently each time; crushing the pieces ruins texture. Listen for cereal crackling slightly as you fold."
- "Mix powdered sugar with turmeric in a big airtight bag. If color clumps, pulse in food processor or sift briskly — no lumps allowed."
- "Add small batches of coated cereal to bag. Shake gently but purposefully — too hard and you’ll powder too thickly; too light and it won’t stick well."
- "Remove coated cereal to wax paper–lined rimmed baking sheet. Spread thin, single layer to avoid sticking."
- "Chill until sugars firm up; you’ll know when fingers touch powder and nothing sticks. About 1 hour but watch cues."
- "Store sealed at room temperature if temp is cool; otherwise refrigerate. Cold dulls flavor but keeps shape longer."
- "If colors seem uneven, toss lightly again before serving. Use parchment or gloves to prevent staining hands."
