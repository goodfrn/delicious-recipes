ingredients:
- "For the crust==="
- "1 1/2 cups digestive biscuit crumbs (can substitute graham crackers)"
- "8 tablespoons unsalted butter melted (or coconut oil for dairy-free)"
- "3 tablespoons granulated sugar"
- "Next layer==="
- "8 ounces cream cheese softened"
- "8 ounces mascarpone cheese softened (replaces half cream cheese for tang)"
- "1/3 cup powdered sugar"
- "1 container (8 oz) whipped topping divided"
- "Next layer==="
- "1 1/2 cups sweetened condensed milk"
- "3 large egg yolks"
- "3/4 cup fresh lemon juice (about 4 large lemons)"
- "Last layer==="
- "1 container (8 oz) whipped topping remaining"
instructions:
- "=== For the crust ==="
- "Heat oven to 345°F; precise temp matters-avoid scorching flavors. Mix digestive crumbs, melted butter, sugar thoroughly until mixture holds when pinched. Press firmly into a buttered 9x13 glass dish bottom. No gaps, no loose edges - helps avoid sogginess later. Bake 15-18 minutes or until edges just turn golden, faint nutty scent noticeable. Cool fully at room temp - do not refrigerate hot crust; condensation ruins crispness."
- "=== For the cream cheese layer ==="
- "Whip softened cream cheese and mascarpone with powdered sugar until lump-free and fluffy. Fold in 1 container whipped topping gently; retains airiness preventing dense slabs. Spread evenly over cooled crust; use offset spatula for smooth finish. Chill briefly while preparing next layer - this helps layering hold tight without mixing."
- "=== Lemon custard layer ==="
- "Whisk sweetened condensed milk, egg yolks, and fresh lemon juice briskly until mixture thickens slightly and lightens in color. Pay attention- too thin means under-whisked; too thick can curdle when baking. Pour evenly atop cream cheese layer, gently spreading to edges. No double-dipping spoons- keep surfaces neat and defined. Return to fridge promptly."
- "=== Final whipped topping ==="
- "Spread remaining whipped topping evenly. Don’t skimp. Edges especially need covering, seal the surface. Chill at least 5 hours or preferably overnight. Avoid shaking or moving dish until set; subtle jiggling indicates incomplete setting. For faster set, pop in freezer 30 minutes but don’t freeze solid or whipped topping loses texture."
- "=== Serve ==="
- "Slice with warm, thin-bladed knife wiped clean after each cut. Clean edges help presentation and prevent crumb spills. Store leftovers tightly covered in fridge max 4 days; crust softens but flavor improves overnight soak."
