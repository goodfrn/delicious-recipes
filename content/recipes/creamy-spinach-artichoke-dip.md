ingredients:
- "8 ounces cream cheese softened"
- "1/3 cup Greek yogurt thick and strained"
- "10 ounces fresh spinach or frozen thawed, squeezed dry"
- "14 ounces canned artichoke hearts drained and chopped"
- "1/2 cup leek soup substitute with mushroom broth concentrate diluted"
- "2 cloves garlic minced fine"
- "1/2 teaspoon kosher salt"
- "1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper"
- "1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice"
- "1 cup shredded Gruyere or Swiss cheese"
instructions:
- "Start with softened cream cheese and Greek yogurt in large bowl; paddle or beat until completely creamy with no lumps."
- "Spinach moisture critical here. Use heavy towels, wring hard, aim near bone dry to avoid watery dip ruining texture."
- "Fold in spinach and chopped artichoke hearts evenly to spread chunks and green through base."
- "Add leek soup carefully—watch salt levels since canned broths vary; mix in minced garlic, salt, pepper, and bright lemon juice for balance."
- "Check consistency, should be thick but spreadable; chill covered for about 55 minutes so flavors marry and dip firms slightly."
- "For hot version, preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit; spread dip in oven-safe dish, top evenly with shredded Gruyere."
- "Bake until cheese melts fully and just begins golden-brown edges, around 20 minutes. Look for bubbling around sides as done indicator."
- "Serve warm right out of oven or cold with crackers, sliced baguette, or fresh veggies."
