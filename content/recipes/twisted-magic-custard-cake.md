ingredients:
- "4 large eggs yolks"
- "1 cup granulated sugar reduced from original"
- "1/3 cup unsalted butter melted and cooled"
- "1 teaspoon almond extract replacing vanilla"
- "1/2 cup all-purpose flour"
- "1/4 cup almond flour replacing part of all-purpose"
- "1 1/2 cups whole milk"
- "1/2 cup coconut milk full fat replacing heavy cream"
- "4 large egg whites"
- "Powdered sugar for dusting"
instructions:
- "=== Prepare pan and oven ==="
- "Set oven to 325F. Butter 8x8 inch square baking dish thoroughly—fat creates crust barrier. No parchment here; butter for browning and release."
- "=== Egg yolk base ==="
- "In bowl, beat yolks and sugar till pale yellow thick cream—1 1/2 to 2 minutes max. Don’t overbeat; want airy but not dry. Sugar dissolves fueling custard."
- "Add cooled melted butter, then almond extract. Blend well. Butter brings richness and moist crumb; extract shifts aroma profile subtly."
- "=== Flour integration ==="
- "Sift both flours together, fold into wet mix carefully. Overworking develops gluten; keep it light but uniform. Almond flour loosens structure and adds nutty note."
- "=== Milk combo ==="
- "Mix whole milk and coconut milk. Slowly drizzle into batter while mixing gently. Too fast can scramble eggs. Coconut adds silkiness and slight sweetness, less dense than cream."
- "=== Egg whites preparation ==="
- "Whip whites in clean, dry bowl on medium-high till stiff peaks form. Look for sharp, glossy peaks that hold shape but are not dry or curdled."
- "=== Folding technique ==="
- "Use large whisk or spatula. Incorporate whites in 3 gentle additions. Fold—do not stir. Preserve air bubbles critical for custard lift. Batter will split visually: thin bottom, fluffy top floating. That’s right."
- "=== Baking ==="
- "Pour batter into prepared pan. No tapping; keep air intact. Place center rack. Oven heat drives custard set from bottom upward."
- "Bake 45-50 minutes but watch cues: top lightly browned, center wobbly but not loose. Jiggle test—slosh means underdone."
- "=== Cooling and chilling ==="
- "Remove, leave on wire rack. Cool completely at room temp before refrigerating 2-3 hours. Chilling firms custard layers, stops cooking."
- "=== Serving ==="
- "Dust with powdered sugar just before serving. Slice into 9 squares—serve cold or room temp. Remove pieces individually with thin offset spatula or butter knife warmed in hot water for cleaner edges."
