ingredients:
- "1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour"
- "1/4 cup whole wheat flour"
- "3/4 teaspoon baking soda"
- "1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar"
- "1/2 teaspoon kosher salt"
- "1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon"
- "3/4 cup melted unsalted butter"
- "3/4 cup granulated sugar"
- "3/4 cup packed brown sugar"
- "2 large eggs"
- "3 tablespoons whole milk"
- "1 1/2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract"
- "Cinnamon sugar mix 1 tablespoon granulated sugar + 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon"
- "Mix-ins for quadrants: Snickerdoodle cinnamon sugar sprinkle; chocolate chunks (added after baking); pistachios + white chocolate chips; crushed pretzels + peanut butter chips"
instructions:
- "Preheat oven 345°F. Spray 13x18 half sheet pan with nonstick spray, set aside."
- "In large bowl, whisk melted butter with sugars. Scrape sides; break brown sugar lumps."
- "Add eggs, milk, vanilla. Whisk thoroughly but no foam."
- "Combine flours, baking soda, cream of tartar, salt, cinnamon separately."
- "Add dry to wet, fold gently just till mostly combined. Avoid overmix; else tough cookies."
- "Divide dough into 4 equal portions (approx 10-11 ounces each)."
- "Put each portion into separate medium bowls."
- "Stir in mix-ins per quadrant: pistachios+white chocolate; crushed pretzels+peanut butter chips; snickerdoodle sprinkle; plain base for chocolate chunks later."
- "Spread each flavored dough into one quadrant on sheet pan. Flatten with rubber spatula evenly; need not be perfect."
- "Dust cinnamon sugar on snickerdoodle quarter; add extra sprinkles on a chosen section."
- "Bake 18-22 minutes. Watch surface: puffy but starting to flatten. Light golden edges. Tap pan if bubble dome too high to settle."
- "Remove promptly. Let cool fully on pan. Then scatter chocolate chunks on designated quarter while warm to melt slightly but keep shape."
- "Cut into 24 squares once completely cooled. Serve or store airtight room temp up to 5 days if no one devours sooner."
