instructions:
- "Preheat oven to 410F instead of 425 to avoid quick burn, line baking tray with parchment; gives clean release and easy cleanup"
- "Slice peppers lengthwise in halves, carefully remove seeds but keep stems intact; helps keep topping in place and adds visual rustic charm"
- "Drizzle a light brush of olive oil inside pepper cavities before filling; prevents sogginess and adds subtle flavor contrast"
- "Spoon about 1 1/4 teaspoons of chunky tomato basil sauce into each pepper half. Thicker sauce keeps moisture in check and avoids watery bake"
- "Sprinkle roughly 1 to 1 1/2 tablespoons mozzarella cheese over sauce. Less cheese reduces grease puddles and helps even melting"
- "Top each with 3 small slices turkey pepperoni, pressing lightly so slices don’t slide off during baking; turkey pepperoni cuts fat, same flavor impact"
- "Pop in oven for 10 to 13 minutes watching cheese bubble fully and turn golden in spots. Edges of pepper should feel tender but not collapsing"
- "Remove from oven when cheese looks alive with browned spots and pepper edges soften but hold shape. Let cool 3 minutes on tray, cheese will firm slightly"
- "Garnish with fresh oregano or chopped basil for herbal brightness"
- "Serve warm directly from tray to keep crispness on bottom"
