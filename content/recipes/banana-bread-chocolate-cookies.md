ingredients:
- "1 cup oat flour"
- "1 cup all-purpose flour"
- "2 tsp arrowroot powder"
- "1/2 tsp baking soda"
- "1/2 tsp baking powder"
- "1/4 tsp salt"
- "3/4 tsp ground cardamom"
- "1/2 tsp ground nutmeg"
- "1/2 cup unsalted butter softened"
- "3/4 cup granulated sugar"
- "1/2 cup packed light brown sugar"
- "3/4 cup mashed ripe banana (~2 large bananas)"
- "1 large egg"
- "1 egg yolk"
- "2 tsp vanilla extract"
- "1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips"
- "FOR FROSTING:"
- "4 oz softened cream cheese"
- "2 tbsp unsalted butter softened"
- "1 1/4 cups powdered sugar"
- "1/2 tsp vanilla extract"
- "Pinch salt"
- "1 tsp finely grated lime zest"
- "Optional: chopped nuts and extra cardamom for garnish"
instructions:
- "Set oven rack middle position; preheat oven to 395°F. Prepare 2 baking sheets with parchment or silicone mats."
- "Whisk oat and all-purpose flours, arrowroot powder, baking soda, baking powder, salt, cardamom, nutmeg in large bowl. Double-check for lumps; sift if needed."
- "Cream butter and both sugars in stand mixer with paddle; medium speed 3-4 minutes until light, fluffy, slight pale sheen on bowl edges. Scrape down bowl halfway."
- "Add mashed bananas. Texture sticky, some lumps are fine. Beat in egg then yolk; finish vanilla."
- "Gradually fold dry mix into wet. Use spatula; no overmix, stop as soon as no streaks. Dough sticky, tacky but holds shape."
- "Fold chocolate chips by hand evenly."
- "Dust hands lightly with flour or use cookie scoop (2 Tbsp). Shape dough into rounds; if too sticky use flour sparingly. Place 2 inches apart on sheets."
- "Bake 8-10 minutes for smaller cookies. Look for cracked edges, puffy, golden patches on tops. Centers will look set but soft to touch; bottoms slightly browned."
- "Cool cookies completely on wire rack while preparing frosting."
- "Whip cream cheese and butter with hand mixer until creamy, no lumps. Add powdered sugar 1/2 cup at a time, vanilla, salt and lime zest last. Beat to spreadable but thick – add more powdered sugar if too soft."
- "Once cookies fully cool, spread or pipe frosting generously. Garnish with nuts and sprinkle cardamom if desired."
- "Store leftovers in airtight container at room temp, 3-4 days max."
