ingredients:
- "4 chicken breasts, skinless and boneless"
- "3/4 cup Greek yogurt, divided"
- "1/2 teaspoon salt"
- "1/2 teaspoon black pepper, divided"
- "1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese"
- "2 tablespoons unsalted butter"
- "1 cup chopped red bell pepper"
- "1/2 cup minced shallots"
- "1 cup sliced cremini mushrooms"
- "3 cloves garlic, minced"
- "Nonstick spray or parchment paper"
instructions:
- "Heat oven to 345 degrees F; not too hot or juices run out too fast."
- "Melt butter over medium heat in skillet; add bell peppers, shallots, mushrooms – hear gentle sizzle. Cook 12 minutes until veggies soften, edges brown slightly. Add garlic last, stir 45 seconds until fragrant but not burned. Remove and let cool."
- "While veggies cool, mix 1/3 cup yogurt with salt and half the pepper in a small bowl."
- "In another bowl, combine remaining yogurt with mozzarella and remaining pepper. The cheese blend needs to be creamy, not too runny."
- "Cut deep pockets in sides of chicken breasts. Use sharp paring knife, slice carefully to keep enough meat to hold stuffing but not pierce through."
- "Divide cheese-yogurt mix evenly; stuff into pockets until just full. Next, spoon sautéed veggies on top of cheese inside pocket, pressing gently but not bursting sides."
- "Coat outsides of stuffed chicken with remaining yogurt-salt-pepper mix, a thin layer locks in moisture and encourages browning."
- "Place breasts spaced on a rimmed baking tray lined with parchment or sprayed to avoid sticking."
- "Bake 33-38 minutes. Look for firm flesh that yields to finger but no pink inside, juices run clear when pierced near thickest part."
- "If edges brown too fast, loosely tent with foil after 20 minutes to prevent burning while interior cooks."
- "Rest chicken 5 minutes after baking before slicing to redistribute juices."
