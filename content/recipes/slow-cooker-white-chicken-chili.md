ingredients:
- "3 pounds boneless skinless chicken thighs"
- "1 14-ounce jar jalapeño salsa verde"
- "3 cans (15 ounces each) cannellini beans, rinsed and drained"
- "1 can (15 ounces) fire-roasted corn"
- "1 large onion, finely chopped"
- "3 cloves garlic, minced"
- "2 cups low sodium chicken broth"
- "1 teaspoon ground cumin"
- "1 ½ teaspoons dried oregano"
- "1 teaspoon smoked paprika"
- "½ teaspoon chipotle chili powder"
- "Fresh lime juice from 1 lime"
- "½ cup chopped fresh cilantro"
- "Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste"
- "Optional garnish ideas: diced avocado, shredded Monterey Jack cheese, sour cream"
instructions:
- "Layer chicken thighs at bottom of slow cooker. No overlapping so heat reaches evenly."
- "Spoon jalapeño salsa verde on top; no need to spread much, let slow cooker do the blending."
- "Add onion, garlic, drained beans and corn over chicken. Sprinkle cumin, oregano, smoked paprika, chipotle powder across surface."
- "Pour broth last—should just cover solids; too much dilutes flavors. Pause. Lift and stir gently to mix. Use spoon edge, slow turns, avoid smashing beans."
- "Cover. Cook on LOW about 5 hours 45 minutes. Watch for bubbling steam, aroma filling kitchen. Chicken should be tender, nearly shreddable."
- "Remove chicken carefully with tongs; place on board or bowl. Let cool slightly to handle. Shred using two forks—chunky bits better texture."
- "Return shredded chicken to pot; stir gently to incorporate without breaking bean skins or causing mashy texture."
- "Add fresh lime juice and cilantro now—fresh herbs lose vibrancy in long cooking."
- "Taste broth. Salt and pepper last; gradual seasoning controls saltiness especially with canned ingredients."
- "Serve with toppings of choice. If sauce too thin, remove lid last 10 minutes on HIGH to reduce. If too thick, stir in broth by tablespoon."
- "Note: If no slow cooker, 4 to 5 hours in covered Dutch oven at 275°F works fine. Watch liquid, stir occasionally."
- "For substitutions: chicken breasts swap fine but dry out faster; add 30 minutes less cooking time and check moistness. Canned white beans good; dried requires overnight soak and longer cooking."
- "Don't rush shredding inside cooker; heat and moisture cause mushy chicken. Remove out, shred firm meat."
- "If salsa verde unavailable, blend tomatillos with jalapeño, onion, garlic in blender; use fresh lime juice to brighten."
- "Avoid over-stirring during cooking or beans lose shape, cloud broth."
- "If spice sensitivity an issue, reduce jalapeño salsa or substitute mild green chilies. Add chopped fresh jalapeño post-cook for heat control."
- "Store leftovers in airtight container, flavors meld after a day. Reheat gently, add splash broth to loosen if thickened."
- "Helps on busy days; prep night before, store all except lime and cilantro, add them day-of."
