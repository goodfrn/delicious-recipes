ingredients:
- "1 1/2 cups shredded zucchini (squeezed dry)"
- "1 1/4 cups shredded carrot"
- "1/3 cup coconut flakes unsweetened"
- "1 cup whole wheat flour"
- "1/2 cup all-purpose flour"
- "1/2 cup packed brown sugar"
- "1 teaspoon ground cinnamon"
- "1/2 teaspoon ground ginger"
- "1 teaspoon baking powder"
- "1/2 teaspoon baking soda"
- "1/4 teaspoon salt"
- "1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce"
- "2 large eggs"
- "1/3 cup avocado oil (or light olive oil)"
- "1 teaspoon vanilla extract"
instructions:
- "Heat oven to 380 F. Grease muffin tin or fit with liners. Not optional. Muffins stick otherwise."
- "Coarse grate zucchini and carrot on largest grater side or pulse short bursts in food processor to avoid mush. Zucchini alone holds water like a sponge. Press firmly in a clean thin kitchen towel or nut milk bag. Paper towel works for small amounts. Squeeze until no more drips. Crucial step. Skip and muffins soggy, fail at crumb."
- "Dump shredded veggies in bowl, set aside. In another large bowl mix coconut flakes and flours. Whole wheat brings structure. Add brown sugar, cinnamon, ginger, baking powder, baking soda, salt. Mix well so leaveners distribute evenly. Clumps ruin rise."
- "Whisk applesauce and eggs in small bowl until frothy. Add oil and vanilla, whisk gently to combine. Pour this wet mix into dry ingredients. Stir until just moistened. Do not overmix. Miss this step and muffins turn tough. Some lumps are fine."
- "Fold in shredded veggies gently, folding not stirring, until veggies just incorporated. Too rough and batter deflates. Batter thick but spoonable. Spoon into prepared cups filling about 2/3 full."
- "Slide into oven. After 18 minutes check tops. Edges pull from pan and muffin tops spring back with light press. Golden brown, smell aromatic with cinnamon and sweet carrot. Muffin spikes a wooden toothpick and watch for tiny moist crumbs, not raw batter."
- "If toothpick wet, bake up to 5 more minutes watching carefully. Oven temps vary."
- "Rest muffins 5-10 minutes in tin before transferring to rack. Hot too fragile and crumbly."
- "Cool fully, store airtight for 3-4 days. Freeze well without sogginess. Thaw at room temp."
- "Substitute notes: Swap all flours for gluten-free blend for allergy. Maple syrup or honey in place of brown sugar but expect wetter batter; reduce oil slightly if so. Use melted coconut oil but expect slight coconut aroma. Greek yogurt can replace applesauce for tang and bite, reduce eggs to 1 if so."
- "No nut milk bag? Use a few layers of clean thin dish towel or even coffee filter layered in a sieve. Patience pressing out liquid, very important."
- "If the batter is too dry add a tablespoon or two of milk or water. Too wet, add a sprinkle of flour."
- "Avoid large chunks of veg. Too big and muffins collapse. Medium coarse shred looks best."
