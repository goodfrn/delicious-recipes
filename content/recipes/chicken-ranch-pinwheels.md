ingredients:
- "8 oz cream cheese softened"
- "1 packet Italian seasoning mix (dry)"
- "1 cup shredded smoked gouda cheese"
- "1/2 cup sour cream"
- "1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce"
- "1/4 teaspoon fine salt"
- "2 cups cooked diced chicken"
- "1 cup smoked turkey bacon diced"
- "3 scallions thinly sliced"
- "4 large flour tortillas"
- "1 teaspoon hot sauce optional for tang"
instructions:
- "===Mix base spread==="
- "Beat softened cream cheese with Italian seasoning, smoked gouda, sour cream, Worcestershire sauce, salt. Use electric mixer or sturdy whisk. Should be thick but spreadable, no lumps, flavors combined."
- "===Fold in proteins==="
- "Add cooled diced chicken, smoked turkey bacon pieces, scallions, and optional hot sauce. Fold carefully by hand to keep chunky texture, avoid overmixing that can make spread gummy."
- "===Spread and roll==="
- "Divide evenly over 4 tortillas, a quarter each. Spread right to the edges to eliminate bare spots which cause unraveling later. Roll tortillas tightly keeping pressure even for compact cylinders. Wrap each roll tightly in plastic wrap. The wrap traps moisture, firms shape, makes slicing neat."
- "===Chill and slice==="
- "Refrigerate wrapped rolls minimum 30 minutes, max hour. The spread firms as it cools; cold fat solidifies locking filling together. Use sharp serrated knife. Saw gently without squashing or tearing to produce clean 1-inch pinwheels. Remove ends where filling is sparse or uneven for better presentation."
- "===Serving notes==="
- "Serve cold or slightly warmed. Leftovers keep well 2 days refrigerated tightly wrapped. Freeze wrapped rolls for up to a month, thaw in fridge overnight before slicing. Sauce or dips can accompany but not necessary."
- "===Common issues==="
- "If filling too loose add more cheese or reduce sour cream next time to firm spread. Overmixing cream cheese base makes spread too loose or grainy. Tortillas cracking when rolling? Warm briefly wrapped in damp towel to soften. Stale tortillas crumble and ruin neat spirals."
