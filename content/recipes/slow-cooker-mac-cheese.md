ingredients:
- "3 cups elbow macaroni uncooked"
- "4 cups sharp white cheddar grated fresh"
- "3 cups smoked gouda grated fresh"
- "5 cups whole milk divided"
- "1 cup half-and-half"
- "5 tbsp unsalted butter"
- "1 ½ tsp smoked paprika"
- "1 ¼ tsp kosher salt"
- "Fresh cracked black pepper to taste"
- "Nonstick spray"
instructions:
- "=== Preparation ==="
- "Grate white cheddar and smoked gouda by hand. Avoid pre-shredded—it’s coated with starch and won’t melt right. If gouda is a block, grate finely; no slices here. Set cheese aside."
- "Spray 6 to 8-quart slow cooker pot heavily with nonstick spray. Butter and cheese mean sticky mess otherwise."
- "=== Assembly ==="
- "Add dry macaroni right into the slow cooker. Pour in 4 cups whole milk and the half-and-half plus butter cubes. Sprinkle in smoked paprika and kosher salt. Stir it all aggressively with a heatproof spatula. Get butter melted into the mix, coat pasta evenly."
- "Before cheese, smell will hint smoky from paprika—good sign. Add cheeses, stir quickly but thoroughly so strands begin melding into sauce."
- "=== Cooking ==="
- "Cover slow cooker and set on low. Let sit one hour undisturbed. Hear gentle bubbling? Good. No burnt smell? Even better."
- "After the first hour, stir well. Cheese will be partially melted, sauce thickening, pasta softening but firm under bite."
- "Cover and cook one more hour but keep an eye. Test pasta doneness with fork. Should be tender but not mushy—not rubbery either. If pasta’s nearly done before hour’s up, switch to warm setting."
- "Use spatula to scrape edges and bottom where cheese can cling and burn if left dry."
- "If sauce thickens too much or pasta soaks up liquid, add ¼ cup warm milk incrementally. Too thick? Milk loosens it. Too thin? Cook little longer uncovered, stirring every 10 minutes."
- "=== Finishing ==="
- "Before serving, taste sauce. Adjust salt and pepper to preference—essential last touch. Season gently to not overdo salt since cheese and butter add plenty."
- "Serve directly from pot. Mac and cheese will continue thickening as it cools. If reheating leftovers, add splash of milk and stir until loose and creamy again."
- "=== Tips & Troubleshooting ==="
- "Switch smoked paprika to dry mustard for less smoky, more traditional tang."
- "Half-and-half adds creaminess without weighing down like evaporated milk. Use evaporated if half-and-half isn’t handy."
- "If pasta underdone but sauce thick, add hot water or milk, stir and extend cook by 10–15 min."
- "Leftover cheese? Freeze for future sauces, don’t toss. Pre-shredded? Fine for topping but avoid in slow cooker itself."
- "Slow cookers vary in strength. Adjust times accordingly, always rely on sensory checks over clock."
