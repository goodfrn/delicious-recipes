ingredients:
- "1/3 cup spelt flour"
- "2 tablespoons brown sugar"
- "1/2 teaspoon baking powder"
- "pinch of salt"
- "1/3 cup whole milk"
- "1 tablespoon melted coconut oil"
- "1/4 teaspoon almond extract"
instructions:
- "Choose microwave-safe mug holding 12 ounces minimum. Smaller mugs cause spillover."
- "Add spelt flour, brown sugar, baking powder, and salt into mug. Use dinner fork or mini whisk to stir dry ingredients thoroughly. Break up any lumps—spelt flour tends to clump more than all-purpose."
- "Pour in milk, coconut oil, almond extract. Immediately whisk thoroughly until no dry pockets remain. Use small rubber spatula to scrape sides and bottom to ensure all flour absorbs liquid. Otherwise, bites of raw flour will ruin texture."
- "Monitor texture. Look for thick batter hugging spoon but still pourable. Adjust milk marginally if too dense."
- "Microwave set at full power for about 1 minute 20 seconds. Listen for quiet popping, edges puffed and set but center slightly jiggling when nudged."
- "Remove carefully; mug will be hot. Surface may be slightly shiny and rising from sides. If underdone, cook another 10-15 seconds but watch closely—overcooked pancake toughens fast."
- "Top with tangy Greek yogurt dolloped on top and handful of toasted pecans for crunch contrast. Alternatively, swap nuts for dried cherries or coconut flakes for texture variation."
- "Eat directly from mug or invert onto plate while warm. Pancake will deflate quickly; texture best fresh out of microwave."
