ingredients:
- "2 cups all-purpose flour"
- "1 ½ tbsp arrowroot powder"
- "2 tsp baking powder"
- "½ tsp salt"
- "1 cup fresh or frozen cranberries"
- "1 tbsp orange zest"
- "¾ cup granulated sugar"
- "⅓ cup vegetable oil"
- "½ cup plain Greek yogurt"
- "2 large eggs"
- "½ cup orange juice"
- "¼ cup pineapple juice"
- "1 tsp almond extract"
- "For glaze:"
- "1 cup powdered sugar"
- "2 tbsp orange juice"
- "1 tsp lemon juice"
instructions:
- "Preheat oven to 340°F. Lightly grease and flour a light-colored 8×4 loaf pan or spray with baking spray."
- "In large bowl, whisk flour, arrowroot, baking powder, salt till blended. Scatter cranberries over this, toss gently to coat then set aside."
- "In another bowl, rub orange zest into sugar to release oils, smell should brighten. Whisk in oil and yogurt until smooth."
- "Add eggs one at a time, whisk lightly. Pour in orange juice, pineapple juice, and almond extract, mix gently till combined."
- "Add wet into dry ingredients. Use folding motion with rubber spatula. Mix until just moist. Batter will be thick and lumpy—do not overwork."
- "Scrape batter into prepared pan. Tap pan on counter to settle, knock out air pockets."
- "Bake 45-50 min. Watch the top color—golden amber is right. If edges brown fast, tent loosely with foil after 30 min."
- "To test doneness, insert toothpick or skewer near center. Should come out with moist crumbs, not wet batter."
- "Cool loaf in pan 15 mins, then transfer to wire rack. Cool completely before glazing."
- "For glaze, whisk powdered sugar with orange and lemon juice. Adjust liquid for thickness—should drizzle slowly but not run off completely."
- "Drizzle over cooled bread, let set 15 mins before slicing."
