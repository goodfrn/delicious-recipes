ingredients:
- "10.5 ounces digestive biscuits or graham crackers crushed finely"
- "1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt"
- "1/4 cup packed brown sugar or granulated sugar"
- "7 tablespoons unsalted butter melted and cooled"
- "24 ounces full-fat cream cheese softened"
- "3/4 cup granulated sugar"
- "1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla bean paste or vanilla extract"
- "3 large eggs"
instructions:
- "Heat oven to 350°F. Spray 9-inch springform pan lightly with nonstick spray. Set aside."
- "Crush digestive biscuits finely in food processor or zip-top bag with rolling pin. No big chunks."
- "Mix crushed biscuits with salt, sugar, and melted butter until crumbs are all coated — butter binds everything."
- "Press about 3 1/2 cups of crumb mixture firmly on bottom and up sides of pan. Use dry measuring cup for tight packing — crust must hold shape."
- "In stand mixer, beat cream cheese with sugar and vanilla on medium speed until creamy and without lumps. Scrape bowl sides to avoid grainy bits."
- "Add eggs one at a time, mix just till combined. Avoid whisking air in — too much air = cracks or bubbles."
- "Pour filling into crust evenly. Smooth top with offset spatula, but don’t overwork."
- "Sprinkle remaining 2 cups of crumb mix evenly on top. Press lightly; don’t overcompress or topping will turn soggy."
- "Bake 70-80 minutes. Edges set firm, pulling away slightly from pan sides; center jiggles gently when pan shakes — like soft jelly. Use instant-read thermometer if unsure: 140-150°F center."
- "Let cool on wire rack till room temp, then refrigerate covered at least 5 hours, preferably overnight. Important: slicing too soon leads to crumbling or messy slices."
- "Remove springform carefully. Run knife along edge before unlatching to prevent sticking."
