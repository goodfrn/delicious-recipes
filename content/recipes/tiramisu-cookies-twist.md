ingredients:
- "2 cups cake flour"
- "1 Tbsp instant coffee granules"
- "1/2 tsp salt"
- "1/2 tsp baking soda"
- "3/4 tsp baking powder"
- "1/2 tsp ground cardamom"
- "1 stick unsalted butter softened"
- "1/3 cup granulated sugar"
- "1/3 cup packed light brown sugar"
- "2 large eggs"
- "1 tsp vanilla extract"
- "1 cup heavy cream"
- "1/2 cup mascarpone cheese"
- "2 Tbsp dark rum"
- "1 1/2 cups powdered sugar"
- "1 tsp vanilla extract"
- "1/4 tsp fine salt"
- "cinnamon and cocoa powder blend for dusting"
instructions:
- "===Dry Mix==="
- "Whisk cake flour, instant coffee, salt, baking soda, baking powder, and ground cardamom in a medium bowl until evenly combined. Cardamom gives subtle warmth; do not skip for the twist. Set aside."
- "===Creaming==="
- "Using stand mixer with paddle, beat softened butter with both sugars on low initially, then medium speed for 3-5 minutes. Look for a pale, thick paste texture. Slow start avoids sugar flying out; scrape bowl edges once light and fluffy."
- "===Egg and Vanilla==="
- "Add eggs one at a time, mixing to fully incorporate each before next. Mix in vanilla extract. Scrape sides to avoid dry pockets; combined batter will look loose but not runny."
- "===Add Dry==="
- "Gradually fold in dry mix using mixer on low or by hand. Mix just until no flour streaks remain. Overmixing ruins tender bite—stop as soon as it comes together."
- "===Scoop and Shape==="
- "Use 2 Tbsp scoop to portion dough onto parchment-lined trays. Six at a time works best for oven space. Lightly press tops with fingers or bottom of glass to gently flatten each mound for even rise and better shaping. Skip if wanting rustic domed look."
- "===Bake==="
- "Place in 350°F oven. Bake 10-12 minutes. Watch edges turn golden before centers puff and just begin to set—cookie still looks slightly underbaked. They firm up as they cool. Sound: subtle crackle at edges indicates readiness. Remove promptly."
- "===Shape Warm==="
- "Right after pulling trays, slide a rimmed glass over each cookie, not touching edges but encompassing. Spin gently to round edges perfectly while warm and pliable. Cool on trays for 3-4 minutes then transfer to rack fully."
- "===Frosting==="
- "In whisk attachment bowl, combine heavy cream, mascarpone, rum, powdered sugar, vanilla, and salt. Beat ~3 minutes until soft peaks form. Overwhipping can break mascarpone. Soft peaks = slightly droopy but holds shape—ideal for swirl piping."
- "===Pipe and Finish==="
- "Fit piping bag with open swirl tip (Wilton 2A or similar). Swirl frosting from center outward on each cookie. Dust tops with cinnamon-cocoa blend—adds color and fragrance, deeper than straight cocoa. Chill briefly or serve immediately."
