ingredients:
- "2 pounds boneless skinless chicken thighs cubed"
- "1 cup mango chunks drained"
- "1 1/2 cups smoky barbecue sauce"
- "2 tablespoons white wine vinegar"
- "5 brioche slider buns"
- "Optional garnish: thin sliced red onions, fresh cilantro leaves"
instructions:
- "===Prepare the base==="
- "Pat chicken dry. Place chicken cubes in slow cooker. Don't crowd — even pieces cook evenly this way."
- "Add mango chunks on top. Mango holds shape better than pineapple, less watery runoff. Pour barbecue sauce evenly over chicken and mango."
- "===Slow Cook Stage==="
- "Cover and cook on low for about 4 1/2 hours. No peeking — interrupts cooking, lets heat escape. Use timing as a guide, check at 4 hours for bubbling sauce edges and tender chicken that peels apart readily with a fork."
- "===Shred and Adjust==="
- "Remove chicken from cooker using slotted spoon or tongs. Place in a bowl. Sauce will be thick and glossy, slightly caramelized from mango sugars."
- "Add white wine vinegar to sauce and stir thoroughly to balance sweetness. Tastes should hit sweet, smoky, acidic notes."
- "Shred chicken finely with two forks. Return shredded meat to the sauce, stir until coated well. Let sit with lid off for 5 minutes so sauce clings into shreds."
- "===Assemble Sliders==="
- "Toast brioche buns lightly on skillet or oven. Toast adds crunch against tender chicken."
- "Pile chicken mixture high on bottom buns. Add optional thin red onion slices for bite and fresh cilantro for herbal brightness if desired."
- "Cover with top bun and serve immediately before sauce cools and thickens too much."
