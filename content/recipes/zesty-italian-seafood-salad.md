ingredients:
- "1 pound peeled shrimp, tails removed"
- "8 ounces cooked octopus, sliced into bite-sized pieces"
- "4 ounces lump crab meat, picked over carefully"
- "1 medium fennel bulb, thinly sliced"
- "1/2 cup grilled sweet red pepper, chopped"
- "3 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice"
- "3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil"
- "1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt"
- "1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes"
- "1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika"
instructions:
- "Bring pot of salted water to boil. Add shrimp, cook just till they turn pink, about 2-3 minutes. Remove immediately to ice bath to stop cooking. Drain."
- "Blanch octopus briefly if frozen; if fresh, thinly slice. Crab meat should be lump, picked free of shells and veins."
- "In large bowl, combine fennel slices with lemon juice and a pinch of salt. Let sit 5-10 minutes to soften fennel’s sharp bite, release aromas."
- "Add shrimp, octopus, crab meat, chopped grilled red pepper. Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle red pepper flakes and smoked paprika."
- "Toss gently. Cover bowl tightly with plastic wrap. Refrigerate at least 11 hours, up to 16. Flavors marry, olive oil thickens slightly, fennel mellows."
- "Before serving, taste for seasoning. Adjust salt, more lemon juice if lacking brightness."
- "Serve cold or room temperature. Great with crusty bread or as light main."

All easy, but done right makes the difference between a sloppy plate and something balanced across textures and tastes."
