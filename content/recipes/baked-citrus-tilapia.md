ingredients:
- "4 tilapia fillets approximately 4 ounces each"
- "1 cup sliced zucchini instead of cherry tomatoes"
- "1 teaspoon coarse kosher salt"
- "1/2 teaspoon fresh cracked black pepper"
- "3 tablespoons olive oil replacing butter"
- "3 garlic cloves minced"
- "Zest of 1 lemon"
- "Juice of 1 lemon"
- "1 teaspoon smoked chili powder replacing paprika"
- "1/2 lemon sliced thin"
instructions:
- "Set oven to 405 degrees F. Use a shallow 9x13 pan or rimmed baking sheet coated with cooking spray to prevent sticking."
- "Arrange tilapia fillets with thinner edges overlapping slightly; prevents dryness at tips but watch for uneven cooking."
- "Scatter zucchini slices evenly around the fish for roasted veggies that soften and caramelize."
- "Sprinkle salt and black pepper on everything, seasoning early helps flavor penetrate."
- "In a microwave-safe bowl, gently warm olive oil until just more fluid—about 20 seconds. Add minced garlic to infuse, stirring constantly to avoid burning."
- "Mix in lemon zest, lemon juice, and smoked chili powder. Pour this mixture evenly over fish and vegetables, ensuring complete coverage for bright, smoky notes."
- "Place lemon slices atop each fillet; they'll steam and scent the fish as it roasts."
- "Roast in the oven 16 to 19 minutes or until fish turns fully opaque and flakes easily when prodded with a fork. Listen for quiet bubbling of juices as indicator of doneness."
- "Serve immediately atop quinoa, brown rice, or cauliflower rice for a low-carb option. The veggies add texture and subtle sweetness."
- "If overlapping fillet edges undercook, separate slightly or rotate pan midway for even heat distribution."
