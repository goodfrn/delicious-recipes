ingredients:
- "2 cups almond flour"
- "1 cup chopped walnuts"
- "6 tablespoons melted unsalted butter"
- "1/3 cup granulated sugar"
- "16 ounces softened cream cheese"
- "3/4 cup powdered sugar"
- "1 cup reduced-fat whipped topping divided"
- "1 21-ounce can blueberry pie filling"
- "Zest of 1 lemon"
- "White chocolate shavings for garnish"
- "Chopped walnuts for garnish"
instructions:
- "Oven to 340°F to avoid over-toast."
- "Pulse almonds and walnuts until crumbly but not oily - small bites, not powder."
- "Combine melted butter and sugar till sandy texture. Clumping means too wet or dry. Add crust mix; stir till fully coated, sugar absorbed."
- "Press firmly into 9x13 pan, use bottom of glass for packed even layer. No gaps or too thin patches - uneven bake."
- "Bake until edges just golden, about 13 minutes. Centers feel set, not wet. Cool fully before next step - warm crust ruins creamy layer adhesion."
- "Whip cream cheese until soft and creamy using paddle or beaters. Fold half of whipped topping gently to avoid deflating air."
- "Add powdered sugar and lemon zest. Taste for balance; adjust sweetness if needed. Spread evenly over chilled crust with offset spatula."
- "Pour blueberry filling atop cream layer. Distribute to cover but keep thickness even."
- "Top with remaining whipped topping - spread lightly so layers can be seen faintly through."
- "Chill in fridge for minimum 3 hours. Overnight best. Settling firm but not frozen; edges slightly glossy and firm, center softly holds shape."
- "Garnish just before serving - sharp knife to shave white chocolate, sprinkle chopped walnuts for crunch and aroma."
