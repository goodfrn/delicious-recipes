ingredients:
- "1 large egg"
- "3 tbsp plain Greek yogurt (sub mayo)"
- "1 cup yellow cornmeal"
- "½ cup white cornmeal (replace half cornmeal)"
- "3 tbsp raw cane sugar (replace granulated sugar)"
- "3/4 cup buttermilk"
- "1/2 cup sparkling water (replace still water)"
- "2 tsp avocado oil (replace veg oil)"
- "1 tbsp honey (twist added sweetness)"
instructions:
- "Preheat oven to 435F. Swirl 2 tsp avocado oil in 8-inch cast iron skillet; stick it in oven. Let skillet soak up heat minimum 7 minutes beyond oven temp. Hot skillet key to crust crackle."
- "In a large bowl, crack in egg. Whisk in Greek yogurt until creamy. Dump in yellow and white cornmeal, raw cane sugar, then drizzle honey."
- "Pour buttermilk slowly, then add sparkling water to loosen batter. Whisk until coarse lumps vanish but don’t overmix — gritty texture is good."
- "Use oven mitts. Pull skillet from oven carefully, tilt and spoon batter immediately into hot oil shimmering on skillet floor. Batter should hiss and sputter — that’s skillet singing."
- "Pop skillet back. Bake 22 to 27 mins. Watch edges bubble and crust darken brown with toasted amber spots. Tap crust — it should sound hollow, not wet."
- "For aggressive crust, broil 3 to 4 mins. Eye crust color: deep copper, almost burnt. Be ready to pull fast or smoke alarm visits."
- "Remove skillet, set on trivet. Flip cornbread skillet-side up right away, quick flip with spatula or plate. Don’t linger in skillet or underside softens."
- "Cut warm or room temp. Crust first bite crunches, inside tender but grainy from cornmeal combo. Slight honey sweetness, tang from Greek yogurt. Not cloying."
- "Store leftovers loosely wrapped. Revive crust by reheating under broiler 1 min or on hot skillet."
