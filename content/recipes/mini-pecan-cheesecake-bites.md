- "0.27 cup melted unsalted butter"

- "3 large eggs"

- "0.3 cup heavy cream"
- "1 cup chopped pecans"
instructions:
- "=== Crust Preparation ==="
- "Oven at 320°F for gentler baking; prevents cracking crust. Line muffin tins with parchment paper liners to avoid sticking; paper aids easy release. Mix graham crackers, brown sugar, melted butter thoroughly. Press 2 tbsp firmly into bottoms of each cup, compact but not overpacked — easy to press finger to check crust firmness."
- "=== Filling Assembly ==="
- "Room temp cream cheese beats fluffy with sugar, 3-4 minutes on medium speed. Scrape bowl edges, essential. Add salt, flour, and vanilla, mix just to combine; you want some air but avoid overworking, which toughens final chew. Sour cream folded in by hand, not beaten - preserves creaminess. Eggs in one by one, low speed, blend to homogenous, there's no rush here; overmixing adds air, causes cracks."
- "=== Baking Process ==="
- "Fill muffin cups roughly ¾ full, leaving quarter inch gap top. Nest muffin tin on rimmed pan; fill pan halfway with boiling water — creates humid even heat bath, crucial to avoid surface cracks and dry edges. Bake 20-25 minutes until centers jiggle slightly — like Jell-O set, not liquid. Avoid overbaking; cheesecake firms more as it cools."
- "=== Cooling and Chilling ==="
- "Let cool 30 minutes at room temp — not fridge yet, condensation ruins surface. Then chill for minimum 60 minutes to firm up fully. If rushed, freezing for 30 can help but texture suffers."
- "=== Pecan Topping ==="
- "Melt butter and brown sugar in saucepan on medium, bubbles start forming and surface darkens slightly (about 3 mins). Add corn syrup, simmer 1 more minute, reduces graininess. Off heat, whisk in vanilla and cream - silky and glossy. Stir pecans in to coat evenly, warm nutty smell peaks here."
- "Spoon warm topping over chilled cheesecake bites; topping thick but pourable. Spread quickly before thickening. Let sit 15 minutes to set before serving, allows topping to adhere properly and cool slightly."
- "=== Notes and Tricks ==="
- "If no corn syrup, substitute with equal maple syrup or honey for different flavor profile and texture. Use salted butter if unsalted unavailable; reduce added salt in filling by half. Sour cream adds moisture and tang; can swap for Greek yogurt but expect slight firmness difference."
- "Avoid cracking by low oven temp and water bath—dry cracked cheesecake ruins presentation. Crust too loose? Add small pinch salt or increase butter slightly. If topping too thin, simmer longer to thicken cautiously, too much heat breaks cream emulsification."
- "Use room temp ingredients for smooth batter. Line tins to release cleanly; otherwise use nonstick spray and chill longer to unmold."
- "Pecans can be swapped with toasted walnuts or slivered almonds. Add cinnamon or cardamom into crust for warm spice note."
- "Visual cues: crust tight and barely changing color when baked. Centers soft with slight jiggle, edges set and pulling slightly away from liners. Topping silky with nut clusters visible but well-coated."
- "These bites hold up well in fridge 3 days sealed. Can freeze individually wrapped, thaw overnight refrigerated."
