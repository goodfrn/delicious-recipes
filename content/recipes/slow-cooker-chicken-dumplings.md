ingredients:
- "2 cups diced carrots"
- "1 ½ cups diced celery"
- "1 ½ cups diced onions"
- "3 frozen skinless chicken breasts"
- "1 can 10.5oz cream of mushroom soup"
- "1 ½ cups water"
- "1 poultry bouillon cube dissolved in water"
- "1 teaspoon dried parsley"
- "1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves"
- "½ teaspoon black pepper"
- "1 cup frozen peas"
- "7 refrigerated biscuit dough cans"
instructions:
- "Layer diced carrots, celery, onions in bottom of 6-quart slow cooker. Mound carefully, no stirring to keep veggies even."
- "Nestle frozen chicken breasts right on top. No thawing needed; keeps them from drying out."
- "Mix soup, water, dissolved bouillon, parsley, thyme, pepper in measuring cup. Pour over chicken/veggies. Try to cover cold meat with liquid mostly; speeds even cooking."
- "Seal lid tight, cook on HIGH 3¾ to 4 hours or LOW 6½ to 7 hours. Around 4 hours HIGH is usually best for tender, shreddable chicken. Peek only if necessary."
- "A sharp fork slide through chicken signals done. Remove meat, use two forks or spoons to shred into bite-size chunks. Return shredded chicken to cooker; fold gently into broth and veggies."
- "Stir in frozen peas last to avoid overcooking, just distribute evenly for color and fresh pop."
- "Open biscuit cans, separate dough. Press/pat each biscuit lightly—don’t mash—into ½-inch thick discs. Cut each biscuit into six pieces, arrange in single layer over crockpot contents. Don’t crowd; extras discarded or saved."
- "Replace lid, cook on HIGH an additional 1 hour 20 minutes. No peeking. Steam traps inside, cooks dough gently. Surface of biscuits turns slightly matte and puffs up when ready. If raw dough patches remain, keep cooking, checking visually through lid."
- "Serve hot. Sauce should be creamy but not soupy; if too thin, remove lid last 10 minutes to thicken by evaporation, stirring gently."
- "Alternative fix: if biscuits cook unevenly, rotate crockpot halfway through final phase. Prevents dough sticking or undercooked spots."
- "If broth too thick before adding biscuits, loosen with splash more water or chicken stock. Too thin, stir in slurry of cornstarch and cold water, cook until slightly thickened."
- "Use fresh thyme over oregano for herbaceous lift without overpowering mushroom base. Mushroom soup adds earthiness balancing savory chicken."
- "If frozen vegetables unavailable, fresh peas added last minute before serving preserves texture."
- "Avoid opening lid during biscuit cooking; steam is essential cook agent. Crack lid once biscuits visually puffy and matte for final confirmation if needed."
