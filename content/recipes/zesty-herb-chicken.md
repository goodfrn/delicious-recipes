ingredients:
- "8 chicken drumsticks rinsed and patted dry"
- "1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice"
- "Zest of 1 lemon"
- "2 tablespoons olive oil plus extra for searing"
- "4 cloves garlic minced"
- "1 tablespoon fresh thyme chopped"
- "1 teaspoon smoked paprika"
- "1 teaspoon kosher salt"
- "1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper"
- "Juice of 1 lime for finishing"
instructions:
- "Combine lemon juice, lemon zest, chopped thyme, olive oil, minced garlic, smoked paprika, salt and pepper in a mixing bowl. Stir until well incorporated."
- "Add drumsticks to the marinade, turning to coat evenly. Let rest 15-20 minutes at room temperature for the acids to slightly tenderize meat without breaking it down excessively."
- "Preheat oven to 425F. Use a cast iron or oven-safe heavy skillet. Drizzle 1-2 tablespoons olive oil in pan and heat on medium-high until shimmering."
- "Carefully lay drumsticks in pan spaced apart to prevent steaming. Hear the sizzle as skin hits the hot oil. Sear 3-4 minutes each side until golden brown and crust forms."
- "Transfer skillet to preheated oven. Roast 35-40 minutes checking meat for firmness and temperature. The internal temp should reach 165F but also press the thickest part to test firmness and juices run clear."
- "Watch for skin turning deep golden, edges crisping. If skin seems unevenly browned, rotate pan halfway through roasting."
- "Remove from oven. Let rest 5 minutes in pan. Squeeze fresh lime juice over drumsticks to brighten flavor and cut richness."
- "Serve hot. Optional garnish: sprinkle additional fresh thyme."
