ingredients:
- "1 box yellow cake mix instead of white"
- "4 large egg whites"
- "¾ cup sour cream in place of buttermilk"
- "1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract"
- "½ cup vegetable oil"
- "Zest of 1 lemon added for twist"
- "½ cup rainbow sprinkles plus extra for topping"
- "Buttercream Frosting: ½ cup unsalted butter softened, 8 oz cream cheese cold, 4 cups powdered sugar sifted, 1 teaspoon vanilla"
instructions:
- "Set oven to 350°F. Fit mini cupcake pan with liners. Paper cups important to avoid sticking."
- "In stand mixer or large bowl beat cake mix, egg whites, sour cream, vanilla, oil, and lemon zest into uniform batter. Scrape sides frequently."
- "Stop mixing as soon as batter looks combined but still thick. Too much triggers tough crumb. Not lumpy; no flour streaks."
- "Sprinkles fold in gENTLY. Use spatula. Stirring too much makes colors bleed, leaving murky spots."
- "Spoon batter into liners filling about ¾ full to keep cupcakes domed but not spilled."
- "Slide pan in oven. Bake about 17-19 minutes. Watch edges turn pale gold and springs back lightly. Insert toothpick—no wet crumbs."
- "Cool cupcakes on wire rack. Warm cakes collapse if frosted too soon."
- "For frosting: whip butter and cream cheese together until fluffy, add sugar gradually, then vanilla. Frost once cupcakes are cold to avoid melting and sliding."
- "Scatter more sprinkles on top for textural pop and festive look."
- "If colors run consult cooler ingredients or gently fold in at end. Use lemon zest for subtle lift against sweetness."
- "Practice recognizing doneness by sight and spring back. Toothpick with crumbs? Needs more time."
- "Leftover frosting can chill to firm up or thin with milk for spreading."
- "Notes: white cake mix swap to yellow gives more flavor. Sour cream thickens texture holds moisture better than buttermilk. Lemon zest aromatic brightens batter."
- "Serving big groups? Bake in shifts. Keep baked cupcakes covered to prevent drying."
