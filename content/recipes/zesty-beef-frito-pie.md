ingredients:
- "1 pound ground beef"
- "3 cloves garlic minced"
- "3/4 cup fresh or frozen corn"
- "15 oz canned black beans rinsed and drained"
- "1 cup smoky chipotle salsa"
- "1/2 cup chili sauce"
- "1 tablespoon taco seasoning"
- "1 1/2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese"
- "2 cups Fritos corn chips"
- "Optional toppings shredded lettuce sliced jalapeños diced avocado sour cream chopped cilantro"
instructions:
- "Preheat oven 365°F convection or 350°F regular. Cast iron heats evenly holding steady temp."
- "Heat skillet medium-high. Toss in ground beef and garlic. Brown thoroughly no pink remaining. Stir often to break meat apart. Drain fat thoroughly to prevent soggy base."
- "Add corn black beans salsa chili sauce and taco seasoning. Stir only till heated through 3 to 5 minutes. Avoid overcooking beans or ingredients turn mushy."
- "Remove from heat. Scatter shredded cheddar evenly over meat mixture. Then crush Fritos lightly over cheese layer."
- "Bake uncovered 18 to 22 minutes until cheese melts and chips turn golden brown crisp on edges. Look for bubbling juices around edges as signal."
- "Remove. Add fresh toppers if desired like jalapeños or avocado slices for punch and creaminess contrast."
- "Serve straight from skillet. Let rest 5 minutes to settle layers before digging in."
