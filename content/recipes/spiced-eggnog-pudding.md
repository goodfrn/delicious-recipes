ingredients:
- "1 packet instant vanilla pudding mix"
- "1 1/2 cups whole milk"
- "1 cup eggnog"
- "1/2 teaspoon bourbon extract"
- "1 teaspoon chai spice blend"
- "2 tablespoons brown sugar"
- "1/4 cup toasted pecans chopped"
instructions:
- "Whisk vanilla pudding mix and milk until lumps disappear. Texture creamy, no streaks. Important: no clumps or grainy pockets. Then blend in eggnog. Should be fluid but richer, thicker than milk alone."
- "Add bourbon extract now, not earlier. Keeps boozy scent vibrant, avoids bitterness. Sprinkle brown sugar slowly, stir. Dissolve it fully to avoid gritty spots."
- "Fold in chai spice. Gentle folding prevents overmixing which breaks pudding gel structure. Incorporate toasted pecans last to retain crunch. Toss pecans first in a little flour if they sink too fast."
- "Place mixture in fridge. No set clock here. Feel pudding after 15 minutes, should jiggle but hold shape with gentle shake. If still loose, chill another 10 or 15, check again. Avoid leaving too long or pudding can thicken excessively and pull away edges."
- "Serve cold. Spoon thick pudding into bowls, scatter extra pecans on top for texture contrast. If pudding feels thin after refrigeration, whip a small amount of cream and fold in to add body."
