ingredients:
- "8 tablespoons salted butter softened"
- "1⁄2 cup packed light brown sugar"
- "1⁄4 cup granulated sugar"
- "1 cup creamy peanut butter no stir"
- "4 tablespoons quick oats processed finer"
- "1⁄4 cup old-fashioned rolled oats"
- "1 teaspoon honey swap vanilla essence"
- "1⁄4 teaspoon fine sea salt"
- "1 cup old-fashioned rolled oats"
- "1⁄4 cup unsweetened shredded coconut added twist"
- "1 cup M&M’s divided"
- "1⁄3 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips"
- "3 tablespoons heavy cream"
instructions:
- "Line an 8-inch square pan with foil shiny side up or parchment paper with overhang for gripping. This saves trouble cutting sticky bars later."
- "Cream butter with both sugars using a hand mixer or sturdy wooden spoon. Stop once pale and fluffy not grainy. This aerates the mixture helping bind texture."
- "Add peanut butter; stir until homogenous paste. Taste for salt balance here especially if using unsalted butter."
- "Mix in processed quick oats, honey replacing vanilla, and salt. Folding is gentle but thorough. Next stir in rolled oats and coconut flakes. Should feel thick but pliable, not dry."
- "Carefully fold in 1⁄2 cup M&M’s without smashing. The candy coating softens easily and will bleed if overworked."
- "Press mixture evenly into lined pan. Use parchment foil edges or spatula to compact firmly to avoid crumbly bars after chilling."
- "In microwave-safe bowl melt chocolate chips with heavy cream stirring every 20 seconds for even melt. Should become glossy, smooth shiny sauce not grainy or overheated."
- "Spread chocolate mixture over oat base. Sprinkle remaining 1⁄2 cup M&M’s on top while glaze is warm for adhesion."
- "Cover tightly with plastic wrap or foil. Refrigerate at least 2 1/2 hours until firm to touch and easily lifted from pan without sticking."
- "Cut bars with sharp knife warmed under hot tap for clean slices. Eat chilled or near room temperature. Store airtight in fridge up to a week."
