ingredients:
- "1 3/4 cups white whole wheat flour"
- "1/4 tsp salt"
- "1/2 tsp baking soda"
- "1/2 tsp baking powder"
- "1 tsp ground cinnamon"
- "1/4 tsp ground ginger"
- "10 tbsp cold brown butter, cubed"
- "3/4 cup packed dark brown sugar"
- "1 large egg yolk"
- "1/3 cup dark maple syrup"
- "1 tsp vanilla extract"
- "Splash milk or heavy cream as needed"
- "1/3 cup granulated sugar"
- "2 tsp ground cinnamon"
- "1/2 tsp ground ginger"
- "For glaze:"
- "3 tbsp unsalted butter, melted"
- "1/4 cup maple syrup"
- "2 oz cream cheese, softened"
- "1/2 cup powdered sugar, sifted"
- "1/2 tsp vanilla extract"
- "Pinch salt"
instructions:
- "Line two baking sheets with parchment or silicone mats then stash aside."
- "In a large bowl, whisk white whole wheat flour, salt, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, and ginger until mixed evenly. Set dry mix off to the side."
- "Brown butter first: cook cubed butter in a small pan over medium. Swirl until nutty aroma, golden brown specks form, and sizzling subsides. Chill browned butter 15 minutes before mixing. This adds depth typical butter lacks."
- "Attach paddle to stand mixer. Beat cold browned butter with dark brown sugar on low speed about 2 minutes until granules mostly dissolved then medium speed 2 more minutes. Texture loosens, faint gloss shows."
- "Add egg yolk, dark maple syrup, and vanilla. Mix until shiny and uniform, scrape sides often. This avoids pockets of raw egg or maple."
- "On low, slowly add dry ingredients. Watch dough tightens, stiffens. If it feels dry or crumbly stop, add splash milk or cream, mix gently until barely combined. Overmix makes tough cookies."
- "Use 2 Tbsp scoop onto plate or sheet, cover, chill 45 to 75 minutes or overnight. Cold dough prevents excessive spreading and helps flavor develop."
- "Mix sugar, cinnamon, and ginger in shallow dish. When dough balls firm, toss to coat every nook in spice sugar."
- "Preheat oven to 345°F. Place coated dough balls spaced on baking sheet. Oven rumbles warming. Bake 12-15 min until edges golden but center puffy and soft. Jiggle gently pan, dough should wobble slightly, not collapse."
- "Cool on sheet 10 min to firm up, crust forms. Transfer to rack to finish cooling completely. Hot cookies too fragile to glaze."
- "For glaze, melt butter and maple syrup in microwave or pan till liquid. Whisk in cream cheese till smooth, then powdered sugar, vanilla, salt until glaze thick but pourable. Adjust powdered sugar to desired consistency."
- "Drizzle glaze on cooled cookies with spoon or piping bag. Let set 10 minutes. Sharp maple hit with creamy tang from cream cheese."
- "Store cookies airtight room temp up to 3 days or freeze dough balls up to 1 month."
