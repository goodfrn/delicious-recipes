ingredients:
- "2 ripe avocados halved and pitted"
- "1 tablespoon fresh lime juice"
- "2 tablespoons Greek yogurt instead of mayo"
- "1 1/2 teaspoons ranch seasoning mix replaced with homemade blend including ½ teaspoon dried dill, ¼ tsp garlic powder, ¼ tsp onion flakes"
- "Salt and black pepper to taste"
instructions:
- "Halve avocados and remove pits carefully. Scoop flesh with a large spoon into a medium mixing bowl."
- "Mash avocados using the back of a fork or potato masher. Aim for mostly smooth with occasional small lumps retaining creamy texture. Over-mashing creates pasty feel, avoid."
- "Add lime juice immediately and stir gently to disperse – acidity preserves green color, slows browning."
- "Fold in Greek yogurt. It sharpens flavor and adds creaminess without heaviness. Avoid over-mixing to keep airy."
- "Sprinkle in homemade ranch blend. Stir lightly until evenly distributed. Taste test. Adjust salt and pepper; ranch mix can vary in saltiness."
- "Serve quickly. If waiting, press plastic wrap directly onto surface to minimize oxidation—discoloration signals loss of fresh flavor."
- "Use for dipping chips or spread onto toasted bread with crisp crust for contrast."
