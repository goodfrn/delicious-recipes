- "1/4 cup fresh lemon juice"

instructions:
- "Dissolve gelatin in boiling water, whisk energetically until no grains visible, glossy texture means done"
- "Add cold lemon-lime soda instead of plain water, fizz and bubbles—fold slowly to keep the sparkle; pour lemon juice and vodka in last, stir gently for even blend"
- "Prep strawberries: slice stems with sharp knife, core by hollowing center with strawberry corer or tiny spoon — false bottom sliced so strawberry stands stable"
- "Transfer gelatin mixture carefully—use food syringe or squirt bottle for precision, steady hands key to avoid overflow and waste"
- "Pop strawberries into fridge, chill 2 hours 30 minutes to 3 hours, jiggle gently after 2.5 hours to check firmness; no jiggle means set"
- "Just before serving: dip top of strawberries in sugar (texture crunch contrast), garnish each with a tiny lemon wedge for brightness and color pop"
- "Serve chilled, cold glass or tray to keep shots cold, avoid warm hands melting delicate gelatin"
