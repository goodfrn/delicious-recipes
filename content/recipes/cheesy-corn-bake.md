ingredients:
- "1 can 14.75 oz creamed corn"
- "1 can 14.75 oz whole kernel corn drained"
- "1 cup corn muffin mix"
- "3/4 cup plain Greek yogurt"
- "1/3 cup melted unsalted butter"
- "1 1/2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese"
instructions:
- "Preheat oven to 355F. Let the oven fully stabilize; don’t rush this part — you want steady heat to coax the casserole’s edges crisping."
- "Grease a 9x13 baking dish well with butter or nonstick spray. Corn mixtures stick otherwise and edges turn ragged."
- "In a large bowl, fold the creamed corn and drained whole corn together, careful not to mash kernels too much—texture isn’t just there for looks."
- "Add the corn muffin mix slowly with a whisk, then spoon in the yogurt. Stir smoothly but don’t overwork; that keeps the crumb from toughening up."
- "Pour melted butter steadily while stirring, so it incorporates uniformly and prevents dry spots."
- "Fold in 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese, holding back the rest for an extra cheese-scorch on top."
- "Transfer the mix to your prepared pan. Spread evenly but don’t press down; light air bubbles help stop it from becoming dense."
- "Sprinkle remaining cheese over the surface for browning and texture contrast."
- "Bake at 355F for about 50 minutes, 5 more than standard. Watch the edges—when they bubble aggressively and top turns golden with darker specks, it’s ready."
- "Remove from oven, set timer 7 minutes rest. Sitting allows the casserole to firm up and avoids a molten lava center that sinks when cut."
- "Serve warm, offer hot sauce or paprika dusting if desired to cut richness."
