ingredients:
- "6 ounces softened cream cheese"
- "3⁄4 cup plain Greek yogurt"
- "2 tablespoons mayonnaise"
- "2 cloves garlic minced finely"
- "1 tablespoon fresh chives chopped"
- "1 tablespoon fresh parsley chopped"
- "1 teaspoon lemon juice freshly squeezed"
- "1⁄2 teaspoon salt adjust to taste"
- "1⁄4 teaspoon black pepper freshly ground"
instructions:
- "Soften cream cheese at room temperature or microwave in 5-second bursts until spreadable but not melted. Too cold means clumps, too warm means separation—aim for creamy smooth."
- "Use a medium bowl. To cream cheese add Greek yogurt and mayonnaise for tang and creaminess. Mix with whisk or sturdy spoon until homogenous. Sounds clinking on bowl—watch consistency."
- "Add finely minced garlic, fresh herbs. Chives and parsley not dried—they bring bright, fresh notes that dry herbs can't replicate. Stir gently."
- "Season with lemon juice. Acidity cuts richness. Add salt and pepper gradually. Taste after mixing to gauge seasoning level; sometimes more lemon or salt is needed. Remember salt wakes up flavor."
- "No cooking involved. Right after mixing you can serve with chips or crunchy veggies. If storing, cover tightly with plastic wrap to avoid skinning or odor absorption."
- "For smoother texture, can whip with handheld mixer for 30 seconds, but manual stirring works fine."
- "Chilling for 30 minutes blends flavors deeper, but dip is ready immediately. Notice cold dip thickens; let sit few minutes to soften before serving if kept very cold."
