ingredients:
- "2 tbsp unsalted butter browned"
- "3 tbsp coconut sugar"
- "0.5 tsp pure vanilla extract"
- "2 tbsp all-purpose flour"
- "1 tbsp almond flour"
- "pinch fine salt"
- "1 tbsp toasted chopped pistachios"
- "1/8 tsp ground cardamom"
instructions:
- "Brown butter over medium heat until foaming subsides and nutty scent fills kitchen. Watch closely—can burn fast. Set off heat, cool slightly."
- "In small bowl, mix coconut sugar, vanilla, browned butter until grainy texture smooths but not melted. Should be creamy but still thick."
- "Sift all-purpose flour, almond flour, salt, cardamom into wet mixture. Gently fold with spatula. Avoid overmixing; stops tough dough."
- "Stir in toasted pistachios last to keep crunch intact. Dough feels soft, tacky. If too wet, chill 5 min. Too dry, a drop water massage into mixture."
- "Scoop dough onto parchment paper-lined plate or oven-safe dish. Shape into rough disk about 3 inches diameter, 1/2 inch thick—uneven edges ok."
- "Preheat oven to 350°F (177°C). Oven rack middle position best for even browning."
- "Bake until edges golden with tiny cracks visible, center set but tender—about 9-11 minutes. Listen: minimal crackle, not popping or sizzle."
- "Cool 5-7 min before tasting. Warm dough will be fragile. If buttery grease pools on surface, chilling helps firm up texture."
- "If no almond flour, fine oat flour works but expect less crumbly mouthfeel; substitute pistachios for chopped pecans or pepitas for nut-free twist."
