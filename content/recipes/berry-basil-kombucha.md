ingredients:
- "3 oz fresh strawberries"
- "3 oz fresh blackberries"
- "10 fresh basil leaves"
- "3 tbsp fresh lime juice"
- "12 oz raw kombucha, chilled"
- "8 oz ginger beer, chilled"
- "Ice cubes as needed"
instructions:
- "Step 1. Rinse berries and basil, pat dry. Roughly chop strawberries, leave blackberries whole or halved if large. Tear basil leaves to release oils; avoid crushing stems to prevent bitterness."
- "Step 2. In a sturdy bowl or large glass, muddle berries, basil leaves, and lime juice. Aim for a coarse puree — press until juices run but some chunkiness remains. Look for deep red-purple color and crushed basil aroma, not pulverized mash."
- "Step 3. Quickly divide this mixture into two mason jars or tall glasses. Notice how juice separates; swirl gently to keep aroma alive."
- "Step 4. Pour 6 oz chilled kombucha into each. Follow immediately with 4 oz chilled ginger beer — bubbles snap and fizz, releasing fresh spice and citrus notes. Ginger beer adds bright heat, balancing berry earthiness."
- "Step 5. Add ice cubes to fill glasses without diluting too fast. Large ice slows melting; clinking sounds signal freshness."
- "Step 6. Stir gently but thoroughly with bar spoon or straw to combine layers. Watch color clouds swirl visually. Taste. Adjust lime or add basil sprig for garnish if desired."
- "Step 7. Serve immediately before fizz fades. Sip slowly, savoring texture shifts and aromatics."
