- "Mix cake batter following box instructions scaled to 18 cupcakes; batter texture should be slightly thick but pourable. - "Arrange cupcakes on platter with decorated side facing up so it looks like bunnies are nose-first buried in grass. Crowd tightly for effect but allow easy serving access."
- "If dabbing black frosting proves tricky, thin it slightly with water or use edible ink pen for details. Color bleeding happens if icing too soft or wet."
- "Chill cupcakes if weather warm to firm frosting but serve at room temp for best texture. Cupcake crumbs and messy fingers inevitable but worth the mess."
