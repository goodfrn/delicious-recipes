ingredients:
- "3 cups cooked shredded chicken"
- "1 cup diced sweet potatoes"
- "1 cup frozen peas"
- "1 cup canned corn drained"
- "1 cup diced carrots"
- "1 can (10 oz) cream of chicken soup"
- "1 can (10 oz) cream of mushroom soup"
- "1 teaspoon shallot powder"
- "1 teaspoon onion powder"
- "1 teaspoon Italian seasoning"
- "1/2 teaspoon black pepper"
- "1 cup heavy cream"
- "1 prepared pizza dough (about 12-inch diameter)"
- "3 tablespoons melted butter"
- "1 teaspoon smoked paprika"
instructions:
- "Warm oven to 380 degrees F. Grease 9x13 casserole dish lightly with oil or butter spray to prevent sticking."
- "In a large bowl, fold together shredded chicken, diced sweet potatoes, peas, corn and carrots. Use fresh or thawed frozen veggies only. Avoid soggy filling."
- "Mix in cream of chicken and cream of mushroom soups. Then add shallot powder, onion powder, Italian seasoning, black pepper. Stir well until uniform. Pour heavy cream last. Texture should be creamy but not watery. Thick enough to hold shape when spread."
- "Transfer mixture evenly into bottom of casserole. Spread flat with spatula. No gaps. Important for even baking and avoiding dry crust edges."
- "Place pizza dough over filling gently. Slowly stretch dough as needed but resist tearing. Dough edges should overlap slightly onto casserole sides."
- "Press dough edges down into casserole rim firmly with fork. Seals filling inside. Prevents boil-over and crisp crust edges."
- "Whisk melted butter with smoked paprika until smooth. Brush all over dough top generously. Brush again near edges. Gives color and subtle smoky aroma that cuts richness."
- "Bake uncovered 35 minutes. Watch crust color deeply golden with occasional crackling sound from butter crisping. Filling will bubble quietly at edges. Touch crust top - firm but springy. Avoid burnt or pale."
- "Remove from oven, let rest 10 minutes before slicing. Filling thickens and settles. Serve warm, not hot, to avoid burns and soggy bottom."
