ingredients:
- "1 box chocolate cake mix"
- "1 cup whole milk"
- "1 package instant chocolate pudding mix"
- "3/4 cup creamy peanut butter"
- "1 cup whipped topping, thawed"
- "4 ounces cream cheese, softened"
- "1 cup chopped peanut butter cups"
- "1/2 cup crushed pretzels"
instructions:
- "Bake cake following package instructions but check doneness by toothpick in center—comes out with moist crumbs, not wet batter. Cool 20 to 25 minutes, test surface touch—firm but warm, slight springiness no longer sticky."
- "Use rounded end of wooden spoon to poke holes approx. 2 inches apart. Don’t punch through bottom layer—holes create pockets for pudding soak."
- "Whisk pudding mix, whole milk, and 3/4 cup peanut butter with cream cheese until glossy and thick. Immediately pour evenly over cake surface so it seeps into holes. Texture resembles thick sauce, adding layers of moisture."
- "Combine whipped topping with remaining peanut butter until streak-free but fluffy. Spread evenly over cake using an offset spatula, hold spatula close to surface for thin layer to avoid mixing pudding layer up."
- "Top with peanut butter cups and sprinkle crushed pretzels for a salty contrast. Refrigerate covered at least 3 hours to set pudding and melding flavors."
- "If cake gets soggy after long fridge time, serve at room temperature 20 minutes before slicing. Use serrated knife to cut without compressing layers."
