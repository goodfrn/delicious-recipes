ingredients:
- "1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour"
- "2 tablespoons unsweetened Dutch process cocoa powder"
- "3 tablespoons dark brown sugar packed"
- "1 teaspoon baking powder"
- "1/2 teaspoon baking soda"
- "1/4 teaspoon salt"
- "1 cup almond milk (unsweetened, room temperature)"
- "1 teaspoon white vinegar"
- "2 large eggs"
- "1 1/2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract"
- "2 tablespoons red food coloring (liquid or gel)"
- "1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon"
- "Butter or neutral oil for cooking"
- "Cream Cheese Glaze ==="
- "4 ounces cream cheese softened"
- "3/4 cup powdered sugar sifted"
- "2-3 tablespoons whole milk"
- "1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract"
instructions:
- "=== Combine dry ingredients first ==="
- "Sift or whisk flour, cocoa powder, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon in a large bowl. Flush out lumps. This ensures even rise and consistent texture. Set aside."
- "=== Prepare wet mix ==="
- "In another bowl, mix almond milk and vinegar. Let sit 3 minutes until curdled, acidic punch activates leaveners. Crack eggs directly in, add vanilla extract and red food coloring. Use a fork to beat until yolks and whites are fully integrated, no streaks."
- "=== Merge wet into dry ==="
- "Pour wet into dry ingredients. Using a rubber spatula, fold gently until just combined. Small lumps? Fine. Overmixing develops gluten, makes tough pancakes. Look for softened batter, still slightly thick, not runny."
- "=== Heat and prep pan ==="
- "Preheat a heavy nonstick skillet or griddle on medium heat. Test heat by flicking water—should dance and evaporate quickly but not burn. Brush with melted butter or spray lightly with neutral oil; avoid too much to prevent soggy edges."
- "=== Cook the cakes ==="
- "Using a 1/4 cup scoop or measuring cup, drop batter onto skillet. Work in batches, leave space for spreading. Bubbles will form and pop on the surface in about 2-3 minutes — key signal to flip. Edges look set and matte, not wet or shiny. Flip and cook another 1-2 minutes until the bottom browns evenly and pancake springs back lightly when poked."
- "=== Serve immediately or keep warm ==="
- "Stack pancakes on warm plates. Repeat with remaining batter, brushing skillet in between as needed. If pancakes stick, increase heat slightly or ensure pan is well-coated with butter/oil."
- "=== Make glaze ==="
- "Place softened cream cheese, powdered sugar, vanilla, and 2 tablespoons milk in a blender or food processor bowl. Pulse until creamy and pourable; add more milk if too thick but avoid thin glaze. Refrigerate until use."
- "=== Dress and garnish ==="
- "Drizzle glaze over warm pancakes. Fresh strawberries, mint leaves, or festive sprinkles add texture and color contrast."
- "=== Tips and troubleshooting ==="
- "If batter looks dry, add a splash more almond milk to loosen. Not enough bubbles after 3 minutes? Pan probably too hot or batter overmixed. Avoid flipping early; premature flipping causes flat cakes. Leftover glaze keeps 1 week refrigerated tightly sealed. Re-whip before serving."
