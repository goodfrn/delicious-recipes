ingredients:
- "4 boneless skinless chicken thighs"
- "1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup"
- "1 cup low sodium chicken broth"
- "1/2 tsp red pepper flakes"
- "1 tsp smoked paprika"
- "1 packet dry ranch seasoning"
- "3 tbsp unsalted butter"
- "1 medium yellow onion chopped"
- "8 oz cremini mushrooms cleaned and sliced"
- "Cooked buttery jasmine rice for serving"
- "Roasted corn kernels, optional topping"
instructions:
- "Heat butter in skillet over medium till foaming, add chopped onion and cremini mushrooms. Sauté stirring often for 5-7 minutes until onions translucent, mushrooms release moisture and edges brown slightly; signals caramelization and flavor development."
- "Place chicken thighs in slow cooker base. Ladle cream of mushroom soup, chicken broth over meat. Sprinkle red pepper flakes, smoked paprika, ranch seasoning evenly. Stir gently to combine spices and liquid but keep chicken mostly intact."
- "Add sautéed mushroom and onion mix into crock pot. Stir lightly to distribute aromatics without breaking chicken pieces."
- "Cover slow cooker. Set to low heat and cook for about 3 hours 15 minutes. After 3 hours, test doneness by piercing thickest part with fork or knife. Should feel tender, juices clear, pull apart easily sometimes. Avoid overcooking; dry rubbery chicken means too long or too hot."
- "Remove chicken. Stir sauce well to mixture smoothness; can add splash water or broth to loosen if sauce too thick or separate."
- "Serve chicken over hot buttery jasmine rice. Sprinkle with roasted corn if desired; adds crunch and sweetness contrast to richness."
