ingredients:
- "3 tbsp oat flour"
- "2 tbsp pure maple syrup"
- "1 tbsp cocoa powder"
- "1/8 tsp ground cinnamon"
- "pinch of salt"
- "1 tbsp dark chocolate chunks"
- "3 tbsp milk (dairy or alternative)"
- "1 tbsp melted coconut oil"
- "1/4 tsp vanilla extract"
- "1/4 tsp instant espresso powder"
instructions:
- "Combine dry: In a microwave-safe mug sized 10-12 oz, stir together oat flour, cocoa, cinnamon, salt, espresso powder and chocolate chunks. Mix until evenly distributed."
- "Add wet: Pour in milk, coconut oil and vanilla. Blend lightly just until no dry patches remain. Avoid vigorous mixing; over-beating can make texture dense."
- "Microwave: Cook on high power starting with 70 seconds. Watch surface closely. Dome should rise, top slightly shiny but not wet-looking. No jiggle when nudged means done."
- "Cool briefly: Let sit 1-2 minutes to complete cooking inside. Cake should spring back lightly under fingertip. If underdone, zap additional 10 seconds increments."
- "Serve: Optional extra chocolate shards or fresh berries on top. Scoop vanilla ice cream or whipped cream for contrast."
