ingredients:
- "1 cup heavy cream"
- "1/3 cup plain Greek yogurt"
- "1/3 cup kefir"
- "1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika optional"
- "1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro optional"
- "1/2 teaspoon lime zest optional"
instructions:
- "Heat cream gently in microwave-safe bowl until warm but not hot, about 45 seconds. Should feel warm to the touch, no steam."
- "Whisk Greek yogurt and kefir together in a separate glass bowl until uniform."
- "Combine warm heavy cream with yogurt mixture slowly, stirring persistently to avoid lumps. Texture will thin, but creamy spots will start to thicken as you stir."
- "Cover bowl loosely with clean cloth or use plastic wrap tented to allow air exchange but keep dust out. Place in warm spot between 70-95°F. Ideal to avoid exposing to direct sunlight or heat above 100°F to preserve bacteria activity."
- "Allow mixture to sit undisturbed for 5 to 7 hours. Look for thickened edges and a creamy custard-like consistency. Texture should be pourable but clingy to a spoon, tactile cue for readiness."
- "After thickening, optionally fold in smoked paprika, cilantro, lime zest for an aromatic twist. Use an immersion blender if blending herbs evenly, careful not to overmix and thin mixture."
- "Refrigerate after culturing. Flavor profiles deepen overnight. Use within 4 days. Always stir before serving to reset texture."
