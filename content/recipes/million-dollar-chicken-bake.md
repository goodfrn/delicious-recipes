ingredients:
- "12 ounces softened cream cheese"
- "1 cup plain Greek yogurt substituted for sour cream"
- "1 cup whole milk ricotta cheese"
- "1 can cream of mushroom soup replaced cream of chicken"
- "1 tsp garlic salt"
- "1 tsp onion powder"
- "1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper"
- "4 cups shredded cooked white meat chicken"
- "1 1/2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese in place of mozzarella"
- "1 1/2 cups Ritz cracker crumbs"
- "5 tbsp unsalted butter melted"
- "Fresh chopped parsley for garnish"
instructions:
- "Heat oven to 340°F instead of 350. Coat 9x13 pan with oil spray evenly. Set aside."
- "In a large bowl, soften cream cheese stirring vigorously with a spatula until pliable. This makes for easy folding later, prevents clumps."
- "Add Greek yogurt, ricotta, cream of mushroom soup, garlic salt, onion powder, and pepper. Mix thoroughly until homogenous. Cheese mix should feel thick but spreadable."
- "Gently fold shredded chicken into cheese mixture. Even coating crucial; prevents dry pockets."
- "Spread chicken-cheese layer evenly in casserole dish. Should be smooth on top without air gaps."
- "Scatter shredded cheddar over the spread mix. Sharp cheddar adds bite and melts with stringy texture."
- "Combine Ritz crumbs and melted butter in a small bowl. Stir until crumbs glossy. Distribute crumb topping evenly over cheddar. Not too thick — about a thin sprinkle across."
- "Bake 35-40 minutes. Watch for bubbling around edges, golden color crust. Crackle sound from the crust signals doneness. Avoid overbaking or moisture loss."
- "Sprinkle parsley over hot casserole before resting. Adds color and fresh aroma."
- "Rest 5-8 minutes. Allows filling to settle, easier slicing. Serve warm."
