ingredients:
- "5 garlic cloves crushed or finely minced"
- "1 1/4 cups finely grated Pecorino Romano cheese"
- "Pinch flaky sea salt"
- "Freshly cracked black pepper to taste"
- "1 tsp red chili flakes (optional for heat)"
- "1/2 cup good quality extra virgin olive oil or avocado oil"
instructions:
- "Snap garlic cloves, crush or mince finely. It must be fresh and pungent. Aroma should fill the air instantly."
- "Mix Pecorino Romano cheese with crushed garlic in a shallow saucer. Don’t dump it all at once — integrate gently to keep texture varied."
- "Add flaky sea salt, cracked black pepper, and scatter red chili flakes evenly. The salt flakes will catch light and add crunch — don’t skip."
- "Pour olive oil slowly on top. Watch the cheese absorb oil, soften. Not too much—just enough to pool slightly without drowning the mix."
- "Serve immediately with crusty bread: a bread with a crunchy crust, open crumb. Toast if stale but avoid soggy edges."
- "If you want, you can let it sit 3-5 minutes for flavors to mingle, but garlic rawness dimishes with time, so plan accordingly."
