ingredients:
- "1/3 cup melted unsalted butter"
- "3/4 cup granulated sugar"
- "2 large eggs"
- "1 teaspoon mint extract"
- "1/2 teaspoon baking powder"
- "1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder"
- "1/4 teaspoon salt"
- "1/2 cup all-purpose flour"
- "1/2 cup almond flour"
- "1 teaspoon espresso powder"
- "15-18 Oreos broken into chunks"
instructions:
- "=== Preparing pan and base ==="
- "Preheat oven to 345°F; lightly grease 8x8 pan or line with parchment, letting edges overhang for easier removal. Melt butter gently, no boiling, set aside to cool slightly. Break Oreos in a ziplock bag with a rolling pin or fist -- big chunks not crumbs. Set half aside for topping."
- "=== Mixing wet and dry ==="
- "In medium bowl, whisk melted butter with sugar until granulated sugar starts dissolving; this prevents gritty texture. Add eggs one at a time; mix thoroughly after each for smooth emulsion. Stir in mint extract."
- "In separate bowl, sift together baking powder, cocoa, salt, espresso powder, all-purpose and almond flours. Sifting catches lumps, aerates the dry mix, prevents pockets. Fold dry into wet gently but thoroughly. Overmixing develops gluten and toughens crumb."
- "=== Incorporating Oreos and baking ==="
- "Fold half the Oreo chunks into batter, not pulverizing, preserving texture contrast. Spread batter evenly in pan. Scatter remaining Oreos evenly on top — concentrated chunks on surface caramelize slightly creating crisp bits."
- "Slide pan into oven, aim for center rack. Listen for faint crackling edges after 25 minutes. Jiggle the pan gently; edges should be set, middle gives slightly but pushes back. If middle is runny, bake additional 5 to 7 minutes. Resist overbaking or drying out."
- "=== Cooling and serving ==="
- "Remove pan; brownies will shrink slightly from sides. Let cool fully at room temperature or chill for firmer slicing. Cut with sharp serrated knife using gentle sawing motion to avoid crumbles. Store leftovers airtight in fridge to maintain texture."
