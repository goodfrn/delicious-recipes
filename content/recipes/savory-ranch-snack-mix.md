ingredients:
- "3 cups Rice Chex cereal"
- "2 tablespoons light olive oil"
- "1 1/2 tablespoons dry ranch seasoning mix"
- "1 teaspoon smoked paprika"
- "1/4 teaspoon garlic powder"
- "1 teaspoon granulated sugar"
- "1/2 teaspoon salt"
instructions:
- "Heat oven to 305F; line large rimmed sheet with parchment or silicone mat - prevents sticking, makes cleanup easier."
- "In wide bowl toss cereal with olive oil until coated but not greasy. Oil locks spices, delivers roasting heat evenly."
- "Mix dry ranch seasoning, smoked paprika, garlic powder, sugar, and salt in smaller bowl. This mix adds smoky, sweet, savory layers."
- "Sprinkle seasoning mix over cereal. Toss gently with rubber spatula or hands until cereal is speckled and evenly dusted. Too rough? Cracks cereal; be gentle but thorough."
- "Spread cereal in single layer on baking sheet, avoid clumps - dimensions matter for crisping."
- "Bake 14-16 minutes total, stir lightly at 8-minute mark. Listen for quiet crackle as moisture leaves. Watch for golden edges forming; no browning allowed or taste turns bitter."
- "Remove when mix has an audible crunch and slight toasted aroma hits, cool fully on sheet. Crisp solidifies as it cools; warm looks ready but can be soft."
- "Store completely cooled snack in airtight container at room temp. Humidity kills crunch fast. Avoid fridge unless sealed tight to prevent staleness."
- "If smoky flavor too intense swap smoked paprika for sweet or cayenne for heat. Oil swap works to sunflower or melted butter - butter gives richer finish but bakes fast so reduce oven temp by 10 degrees and check sooner."
- "If spice mix isn't dry enough or clumps, sift or grind finer to prevent uneven coating."
- "Baking times vary by oven; trust sound and look more. Burnt cereal ruins whole batch; watch vigilantly near end."
