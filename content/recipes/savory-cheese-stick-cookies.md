ingredients:
- "1 1/4 cups smoked gouda cheese grated"
- "1/4 cup parmesan cheese freshly grated"
- "1/3 cup cream cheese softened"
- "1/2 teaspoon salt"
- "1/2 teaspoon onion powder"
- "1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika"
- "1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour"
instructions:
- "Set oven to 345F. Line baking sheet with parchment or silicone mat. Grate cheeses finely using the smallest holes on the box grater or a microplane. Avoid clumps. Fine shreds melt smoother."
- "Cream softened cream cheese with grated cheeses in a mixer bowl. Blend until no dry lumps. Add salt, onion powder, smoked paprika. Mix until well combined. Gradually dump in flour in thirds. Mix low speed. Look for dough that holds together but is not sticky. Avoid overmixing; tough cookies start here."
- "Using a generous 1 1/2 tablespoon scoop, dollop dough on pan spaced about 2 inches apart. Wet fork tines and press gently but firmly into each mound for pattern. Wetting fork avoids sticking, keeps marks sharp."
- "Bake 18-28 minutes until tops turn golden and smell pungent. Listen for faint crackles from butter melting. Pull sooner for soft centers, longer for all-around crisp. Edges should darken but not burn. Let rest 10 minutes on hot tray—cookies firm up catching residual heat."
- "Cool completely on wire rack if storing. Store in airtight container at room temp up to 5 days. Freeze extra dough or baked cookies wrapped snugly for up to 3 months. Thaw at room temp before use."
