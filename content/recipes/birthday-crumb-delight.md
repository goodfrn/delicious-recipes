ingredients:
- "2 cups crushed vanilla sandwich cookies, fine crumbs"
- "7 tablespoons melted unsalted butter"
- "16 ounces cream cheese, softened"
- "3/4 cup powdered sugar"
- "1 teaspoon pure almond extract"
- "1 cup frozen whipped topping, thawed"
- "1/3 cup colorful sprinkles, divided"
- "2 packages instant vanilla pudding mix (3.4 ounces each)"
- "3 cups cold whole milk"
- "1 cup frozen whipped topping, thawed"
- "1 cup whipped topping for garnish"
- "Extra sprinkles for topping"
instructions:
- "===Mixing the base==="
- "Pulse crushed cookies in food processor until fine crumbs. Avoid big chunks; texture matters. Add melted butter; pulse until clumps form. Should hold shape when pinched. Press firmly into greased 9x9 baking dish bottom. Get even, tight layer. Pop in freezer 8 minutes—to set, prevents sogginess later."
- "===Cream cheese layer==="
- "Beat cream cheese, powdered sugar, and almond extract, not vanilla for a twist, until fluffy. At least 5 minutes using mixer at medium-high speed. Incorporate 1 cup thawed whipped topping. Fold in 1/4 cup sprinkles gently. Spread evenly over chilled crust. Return to freezer 12 minutes, not longer; must be firm, not frozen rock."
- "===Pudding layer==="
- "Whisk pudding mixes with cold milk and 1 cup thawed whipped topping. Should thicken in 3–4 minutes. Watch for glossy sheen; that's peak consistency. Pour carefully over cream cheese layer; work fast but gently to avoid mixing layers. Refrigerate 35 minutes. Set but still jiggly. Don’t rush or pudding separates and weeps."
- "===Finishing touch==="
- "Spread remaining whipped topping with spoon back to create swoops—pull up and down gently, don’t press hard. Texture gives height, interest. Scatter leftover sprinkles liberally. Return dish to fridge until serving time; minimum 45 minutes if layered properly. Store covered to avoid absorbing fridge smells."
- "===Tips & swaps==="
- "Vanilla pudding can swap for chocolate or banana instant for a flavor spin. Use cream cheese or mascarpone depending on tang preference. Frozen whipped topping can be fresh whipped cream but stabilize if makes ahead longer than 2h. If crust too crumbly, add 1 tbsp extra melted butter; too greasy means less butter next time. Chill times approximate; use visual cues—firm crust, creamy but set layers, swooped topping with light peaks."
