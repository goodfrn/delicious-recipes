ingredients:
- "2 1/2 cups cake flour"
- "1/2 teaspoon fine salt"
- "1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder"
- "1 3/4 cups granulated sugar"
- "1 cup unsalted butter softened"
- "3 large eggs"
- "2 teaspoons vanilla extract"
- "1 1/2 teaspoons red gel food coloring"
- "1 cup cultured buttermilk substitute (1 cup milk plus 1 tablespoon lemon juice)"
- "1 tablespoon lemon juice"
- "1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder"
instructions:
- "Preheat oven 345°F. Prep two 8-inch pans: grease thoroughly, dust with flour tapping out excess. Avoid waxed paper liners – cake clings better to metal."
- "Whisk cake flour, salt, and cocoa powder till evenly combined. Sifting helps avoid lumps and ensures tender crumb."
- "Cream butter and sugar on medium speed till pale and fluffy. Around 3 minutes. Don’t rush – this traps air, adding lift."
- "Add eggs, one by one, mixing slowly. Scrape sides often. Incorporate vanilla and red gel coloring cautiously. Splatter risk high if too fast. Gel concentrates color without thinning batter."
- "Combine buttermilk substitute, lemon juice, and baking powder. Gives acidity and leavening. Don't mix long – just blend until homogenous."
- "Alternate adding dry mix and wet acid blend to butter mixture: start and end with dry. Fold gently between additions. Overmixing develops gluten – tough cake."
- "Batter thick, velvety. Split evenly between pans, smooth tops with spatula. Light tap pans on counter to release air bubbles."
- "Bake around 25 minutes. Watch color edges pulling away from pan. Toothpick inserted off-center should come out with moist crumbs, not batter."
- "Cool 15 minutes in pans, then turn onto wire racks. Avoid disruptive removal which can break tender crusts."
- "Frost only when cool to touch. Cream cheese frost or tangy mascarpone brace against sweet crumb."
- "Store loosely covered. Cake will dry quickly without moisture shield."
