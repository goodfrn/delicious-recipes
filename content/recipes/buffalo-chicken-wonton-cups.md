ingredients:
- "24 wonton wrappers"
- "8 ounces cream cheese softened"
- "1/3 cup Frank's red hot wing sauce"
- "1 1/2 cups cooked shredded chicken"
- "1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese"
- "1/2 cup finely diced celery"
- "2 tablespoons chopped green onion"
- "1 1/2 teaspoons apple cider vinegar"
instructions:
- "Heat oven to 345°F. Press one wonton wrapper into each well of a mini muffin pan, crimp edges to form little cups. No tears — handle gently. Set aside."
- "Microwave softened cream cheese in a medium bowl until just melty. Stir in hot wing sauce thoroughly; no lumps. Add shredded chicken, fold until coated evenly."
- "Spoon about a heaping tablespoon of the buffalo chicken mix into each wonton cup. Sprinkling on feta cheese, not too much or it overwhelms."
- "Bake cups 12–14 minutes watching for golden-brown edges on the wonton. Crisp but not burnt. You'll hear a slight crackle as they finish."
- "While baking, stir celery and sliced green onion with apple cider vinegar in a small bowl. Let marinate—adds brightness, balances spice."
- "After baking, cool briefly, then scatter the celery mixture atop each cup just before serving. Avoid sogginess. Serve warm."
