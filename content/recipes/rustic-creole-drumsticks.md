ingredients:
- "3 Tbsp canola oil instead of vegetable oil"
- "1 medium red onion diced coarse"
- "1 green bell pepper chopped"
- "1 bunch scallions, white and green parts sliced into 1 inch pieces"
- "1 14 oz can fire-roasted diced tomatoes replace plain canned tomatoes"
- "1 tsp smoked paprika substitute for cayenne"
- "1 tsp dried thyme"
- "½ tsp marjoram"
- "6 chicken drumsticks patted dry"
- "½ tsp kosher salt"
- "½ tsp freshly ground black pepper"
- "8 oz andouille sausage sliced into ½ inch rounds"
- "Cooked brown rice to serve"
- "Optional twist: 1 tsp chipotle in adobo stirred into sauce for smoky heat"
instructions:
- "Heat canola oil over medium heat in heavy-bottomed Dutch oven or large pot. Toss in red onion, bell pepper, scallions. Let sauté undisturbed 3 minutes till edges caramelize, then stir. Softening but some bite remains, about 5–6 minutes total. Aromatic, sweet, faint crunch."
- "Add fire-roasted tomatoes, smoked paprika, thyme, marjoram. Stir, break down tomatoes with wooden spoon. Simmer gently with lid tilted for 25 minutes. Sauce thickens slowly, glossy sheen, sharp tomato tang mellowed."
- "Meanwhile, preheat oven to 355°F. Dry chicken drumsticks thoroughly with paper towels to encourage crisping. Season evenly with kosher salt, black pepper. Use kosher salt for better grip on skin over table salt."
- "Heat a large cast iron skillet over medium-high. Brown chicken in two batches, avoid crowding. Position skin side down first, press slightly to maximize contact. Each batch about 6 minutes, rotate pieces for even golden crust. Sizzling sound drops when ready. Avoid steaming by letting pan recover heat between batches."
- "Transfer browned drumsticks to 9×13 inch roasting pan large enough for everything. Nestle andouille sausage rounds among chicken pieces."
- "Pour thickened vegetable-tomato sauce over chicken and sausage evenly. Some sausage fat should bleed into sauce at this point, blending flavors."
- "Roast uncovered at 355°F for 35 minutes. Observe chicken skin pulling back, juices bubbling, mild charring spots on sausage edges. Flip drumsticks once halfway if desired for even browning."
- "Check chicken temp with instant read probe in thickest part but away from bone. 165°F safe zone. Juices run clear, no pink near bone, skin feels taut but not dry."
- "Rest chicken 5 minutes before serving. Spoon generous portions over warm brown rice, letting sauce soak in."
- "Leftover sauce reheat slowly on stove, thin with splash broth if too thick. Sauce freezes well, good for quick meals."
