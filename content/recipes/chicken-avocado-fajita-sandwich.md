ingredients:
- "1 large ripe avocado"
- "1 ½ tablespoons fresh lime juice"
- "⅓ cup plain Greek yogurt"
- "1 cup cooked shredded chicken"
- "½ cup chopped cherry tomatoes"
- "¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro"
- "1 medium red bell pepper, thinly sliced"
- "1 medium yellow onion, thinly sliced"
- "1 small green chili (or mild serrano), finely sliced"
- "1 teaspoon ground cumin"
- "1 teaspoon smoked paprika"
- "¾ teaspoon garlic powder"
- "1 teaspoon sea salt"
- "1 ½ tablespoons olive oil"
- "4 whole wheat sandwich thins"
- "1 ½ cups baby spinach leaves"
instructions:
- "=== Chicken Salad ==="
- "Scoop avocado into bowl, mash unevenly—don’t whip it smooth. Add lime juice and Greek yogurt to keep it creamy without watery. Chunky texture good. Fold in shredded chicken, cherry tomatoes, and cilantro until chicken coated but not drenched."
- "=== Sizzling Veggies ==="
- "In separate bowl, toss red pepper strips, sliced onion, and green chili with cumin, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and olive oil. Evenly coated, but not swimming."
- "Heat skillet over medium heat. When hot, add veg mix. Hear that sizzle? Stir every 90 seconds. Cook about 5-6 minutes. Look for softened, slightly blistered edges and caramelized tips on onions. Should still hold shape, not mushy."
- "=== Build Sandwich ==="
- "Split sandwich thins horizontally. Lay down a handful of fresh spinach—raw crunch offsets warm dressing. Spoon chicken avocado salad over spinach, then pile with warm fajita vegetables. Don’t overload, sandwich integrity matters."
- "Serve immediately or chill chicken salad separately for up to 4 hours. Warm veggies just before serving to retain texture."
