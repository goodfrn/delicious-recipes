ingredients:
- "2 cups boxed carrot cake mix"
- "1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt (replaces pudding mix)"
- "1 teaspoon ground cinnamon"
- "1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg"
- "1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom (replaces cloves)"
- "3 large eggs"
- "1/2 cup vegetable oil"
- "2/3 cup water"
- "1 1/2 cups grated carrots (fresh, not canned)"
- "For frosting"
- "1/2 cup unsalted butter softened"
- "4 ounces cream cheese softened"
- "4 ounces mascarpone cheese softened (adds richness)"
- "1/4 teaspoon fine sea salt"
- "1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract"
- "3 1/2 cups powdered sugar, sifted"
- "Optional toppings"
- "Toasted pecans"
- "Candied ginger pieces"
- "Freshly grated carrot"
- "Green and orange buttercream for decorating mini carrots"
instructions:
- "=== Cake Base ==="
- "Dial oven to 320 degrees°F. Spray an 11 by 17 inch rimmed sheet pan with nonstick spray. Use baking spray with flour if you have it; prevents sticking better."
- "In a large bowl sift together carrot cake mix, cinnamon, nutmeg and cardamom. The cardamom adds a warm floral lift unlike cloves. Skip lumps; no gritty pockets."
- "In stand mixer or with hand mixer on low, combine dry mix with oil, water, Greek yogurt and eggs. Beat gently for 2 minutes till just uniform. Overmixing toughens crumb; subtle folds."
- "Hand fold grated carrots last. Even distribution prevents soggy pockets. Don't pulverize carrots; texture contrast is key."
- "Pour batter into prepared pan. Spread evenly and tap pan lightly on counter to settle air bubbles."
- "Bake 27-30 minutes. Cake surface should be lightly browned and edges just pulling away from pan sides. Toothpick inserted near center comes out with moist crumbs but no wet batter."
- "Cool completely at room temp uncovered. Once cooled, chill in fridge for 90 minutes minimum. Cooling firms cake for cleaner cutting and neater stacks."
- "=== Assembly ==="
- "Remove chilled cake from fridge. Spray 2.5 inch round cutter with cooking spray. Cut cake circles tightly side-by-side to minimize waste. Work gently; cake is tender. Transfer carefully to parchment."
- "=== Frosting ==="
- "Beat butter, cream cheese and mascarpone with salt until creamy and light. Don’t rush this step; air incorporation means fluffier frosting, easier piping."
- "Add vanilla extract. Incorporate powdered sugar gradually, mixing on low to avoid clouding counters. Increase to medium speed till frosting is light, thick, and spreadable."
- "Fill piping bag fitted with a large star or round tip, or use offset spatula for rustic look."
- "Place large dollop of frosting on 12 of the cake circles. Sandwich gently with remaining cake discs. Don’t press too hard or frosting squeezes out."
- "Pipe a decorative generous swirl on top of each sandwich. Garnish with toasted pecans or candied ginger pieces if desired. For visual fun, pipe tiny carrots using orange and green buttercream."
- "Serve chilled or just after resting 10 minutes at room temp. Cake stays moist with vibrant spicy notes; frosting tang cuts richness."
- "Leftovers? Store in airtight container refrigerated up to 4 days. If cake seems dense next day, warm briefly to revive softness."
