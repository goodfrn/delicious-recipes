ingredients:
- "2 1/4 cups vanilla cookie crumbs"
- "1 1/2 tablespoons all-purpose flour"
- "1/3 cup granulated sugar"
- "1/4 teaspoon fine sea salt"
- "7 tablespoons unsalted butter melted, cooled"
- "3 1/4 cups full-fat cream cheese room temperature"
- "1 cup granulated sugar"
- "3 large eggs room temperature"
- "1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract"
- "3/4 cup sour cream"
- "1 tablespoon lemon zest finely grated"
- "3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice"
- "1/2 cup lemon curd"
- "Optional garnish whipped cream, fresh lemon slices"
instructions:
- "Heat oven to 350°F. Use baking spray on 9 inch springform pan with 2 inch sides. Set pan aside."
- "In medium bowl mix cookie crumbs, flour, sugar, salt. Stir in butter until crumbs look wet but not greasy. Press mixture firmly into pan bottom and about 1 inch up sides. Level surface. Put aside."
- "Using a large bowl, beat cream cheese with sugar until glossy and no lumps. Important to beat thoroughly now for texture, but keep speed medium low."
- "Add eggs one at a time. Stop mixer between additions to scrape sides. Avoid adding air. Too much air means cracks later."
- "Stir in vanilla, sour cream, lemon zest, lemon juice. Mix just until fully combined. Don't overbeat or batter looks shiny and too airy - risk bubble cracks."
- "Pour batter atop crust. Tap pan gently on counter twice to release any large air bubbles."
- "Bake about 55 minutes. Edges should look set and firm. Center stays slightly wobbly but not liquid. Avoid any browning on top. Dark spots or cracks mean it baked too long or oven hot."
- "Turn off oven. Prop door open 1.5 inches with wooden spoon or folded towel. Let cheesecake cool slowly for 1 hour inside oven. Slow cooling reduces cracks caused by shrinkage."
- "Remove from oven. Cool on rack another hour. Surface should be cool to touch and batter fully firm now."
- "Spread lemon curd evenly on top in thin layer. This hides any surface cracks or imperfections like a veil. Look for lemon curd with smooth glossy texture. If too thick, soften by stirring gently before spreading."
- "Refrigerate uncovered at least 2 to 4 hours. Firm chill needed to set body and flavors to meld."
- "To serve, loosen sides with thin flexible knife. Carefully release springform sides. Garnish with whipped cream dollops and thin lemon slices if desired."
